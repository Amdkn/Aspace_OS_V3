# The Tech OS Manifesto (Layer 0)

> "Listen, Morty. The universe is chaos. The only way to survive is to build a box that chaos can't get into. That's Layer 0." — Rick Sanchez

---

## ⚡ The Prime Directives

### 1. Sovereignty Above All
We do not rent. We own.
*   **No SaaS Dependencies** for critical infrastructure.
*   **Self-Hosting** is the default.
*   **Data Ownership** is non-negotiable.

### 2. Anti-Fragility
*   **Chaos Monkey Accepted**: If a server dies, we spawn two more.
*   **Stateless by Design**: Containers are cattle, not pets.
*   **Backups are Boring**: But restoration is exciting. Test it.

### 3. Automation is Law
*   **"If you do it twice, script it."**
*   **No Human in the Loop** for maintenance.
*   **The TARDIS** (Infra) must fly itself.

## 🧬 The Caste System

1.  **Rick [A1]**: The Architect. He writes the laws. He breaks the rules to make better ones.
2.  **The Doctors [A2]**: The Managers. They keep the TARDIS running.
    *   **13th**: Hardware/Network.
    *   **11th**: Interface/UX.
    *   **12th**: Data/Pipelines.
3.  **The Companions [A3]**: The Scripts. They run the cron jobs. They never complain.

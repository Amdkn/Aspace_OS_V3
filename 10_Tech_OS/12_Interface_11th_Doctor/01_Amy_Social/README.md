# 01_Amy_Social (The Storyteller)

**The Social & Narrative Interface.**
Amy is the heart. She turns data into stories.
Inspired by `os.ryo.lu` (Desktop Window) + `WorkAdventure` (Living Room Bot).

---

## 🧚♀️ Agent: Amy Pond [A3]
*   **Role**: **Social CRM & Communication Aggregator**.
*   **Archetype**: The Girl Who Waited.
*   **Mission**: "Come along, Pond." (Connect the dots).

## 🖥️ The Interface (Web Desktop)
*   **App**: `Amy_Network.exe`
*   **Function**: A unified feed of Email, Telegram, LinkedIn.
*   **UX**: Chat bubbles, warm colors (Orange/Red), handwritten fonts.
*   **Feature**: **Journal de Bord**. Daily summary of interactions.

# J02 — Jerry Bio: LD03 Vitality + LD04 Cognition

## AREA STANDARD — HARD SAFETY CONSTRAINT LAYER

> **Purpose**: This document is the operational hard-safety layer for Jerry Bio.
> It does NOT generate growth. It PREVENTS growth from destroying the biological and cognitive substrate on which all other Jerry areas depend.
> Tone: clinical, threshold-driven, non-negotiable.
> This area owns the STOP signals. All other Jerry areas own the GO signals.

---

## 1. Area Identity

### 1.1 What Jerry Bio OWNS

- **Hard stop authority** over all Jerry areas when vitality/cognition thresholds are breached
- **Sleep architecture** tracking and threshold enforcement
- **HRV (Heart Rate Variability)** as the primary autonomic resilience metric
- **Cognitive load** monitoring and ceiling enforcement
- **Exercise frequency** minimums as a metabolic floor
- **Learning velocity** gates that throttle LD01 business expansion
- **Beth HALT veto** escalation and enforcement protocol
- **Recovery prescription** authority when ORANGE signals appear

### 1.2 What Jerry Bio DOES NOT OWN

- Revenue metrics, growth targets, business expansion decisions
- Marketing, sales, product development
- Relationship-building or social capacity
- Creative output, writing, ideation (these are outputs of LD04, not inputs)
- Motivation, discipline, willpower (these are LD04 downstream effects)
- Any GO decision for any Jerry area — Jerry Bio only issues STOP

### 1.3 Ownership Boundary Rule

```
IF a metric lives in another Jerry area → Jerry Bio does NOT touch it
IF a metric threatens LD03 or LD04 substrate → Jerry Bio ACTS regardless of area ownership
```

## 2. Biological Stack (LD03 — Vitality)

### 2.1 Sleep Architecture Rules

**Primary Reference**: *Why We Sleep*, Matthew Walker

**Hard Rules**:
- **Minimum sleep duration**: 7h/night baseline; 8h/night for high-cognitive-load days
- **Sleep window**: Fixed ±30min (e.g., 22:30–06:30). Variance >45min triggers ORANGE review.
- **No screens 60min before sleep onset** — enforced as environmental constraint, not preference
- **Cool room temperature**: 18–20°C (65–68°F) per Walker sleep optimization protocol
- **Consistent wake time** — the single most enforceable anchor. Even one deviation resets recovery debt.

**Threshold Table**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Sleep duration | ≥7h | 6–6.9h | <6h |
| Sleep onset latency | <20min | 20–40min | >40min |
| Nightly awakenings | 0–1 | 2–3 | >3 or any >15min |
| Subjective quality (1–10) | ≥7 | 5–6 | <5 |

### 2.2 Respiratory & Breath Optimization

**Primary Reference**: *Breath*, James Nestor

**Hard Rules**:
- **Nasal breathing** is the default state — oral breathing is a sign of dysfunction, not preference
- **CO2 tolerance training**: 30s breath holds after exhale, 4–5×/day, as a measurable resilience marker
- **Exhale-to-inhale ratio**: Minimum 2:1 for autonomic nervous system downregulation (Box 2-4 breathing)
- **Volumetric breathing**: 5–6L/min tidal volume target; monitored via breath count during sleep

### 2.3 Cardiovascular & Metabolic Health

**Primary Reference**: *Outlive*, Peter Attia

**Hard Rules**:
- **VO2max**: Tested quarterly. Minimum threshold for Jerry Bio clearance: 35 mL/kg/min.
- **RHR (Resting Heart Rate)**: Tracked weekly. ORANGE >5bpm increase from baseline. RED >10bpm increase.
- **HRV (Heart Rate Variability)**: Primary daily metric — see threshold table below.
- **Aerobic zone 2 training**: Minimum 150min/week at 60–70% max HR (Zone 2 per Attia)
- **Zone 5 sprints**: Minimum 1×/week — required for CV resilience and cognitive blood flow

**HRV Threshold Table**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Resting HRV (ms) | ≥65ms | 60–64ms | <60ms |
| HRV trend over 7d | Stable/rising | Declining 3–5d | Declining 7+d |
| HRV SDANN (24h) | >50ms | 40–50ms | <40ms |

### 2.4 Movement & Structural Integrity

**Primary References**: *Bigger Leaner Stronger* (Matthews), *Becoming a Supple Leopard* (Starrett)

**Hard Rules**:
- **Resistance training**: Minimum 2×/week, full-body splits or upper/lower splits
  - Volume: 10–20 working sets/week per muscle group
  - Progressive overload: Minimum 1 variable (weight/reps/sets) progressed weekly
- **Mobility work**: 10–15min daily — hip flexors, thoracic spine, ankles, shoulders
  - Kelly Starrett pre-rehab protocol for any joint with <90% ROM vs. contralateral side
- **Daily step minimum**: 7,000 steps (non-negotiable floor, not target)
- **Posture resets**: Every 30min during seated work — standing, walking, neck retraction

**Exercise Threshold Table**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Resistance sessions/week | ≥2 | 1 | 0 |
| Zone 2 min/week | ≥150min | 75–149min | <75min |
| Zone 5 sprints/week | ≥1 | 0 (any gap) | N/A |
| Daily steps | ≥7,000 | 5,000–6,999 | <5,000 |

## 3. Cognitive Stack (LD04 — Cognition)

### 3.1 Learning Acquisition Protocol

**Primary References**: *Learning How to Learn* (Oakley/Sejnowski), *Uncommon Sense Teaching* (Oakley/Sejnowski)

**Hard Rules**:
- **Daily focused learning blocks**: Minimum 45min, maximum 90min (diminishing returns beyond 90min per Oakley)
- **Spaced repetition**: Any new concept must be retrieved from memory within 24h, then at 3-day, 7-day, 21-day intervals
- **Interleaving**: Never practice one skill to mastery — interleave with a different skill in the same session
- **Deep work blocks**: Minimum 90min of uninterrupted cognitive work per day for LD04 to remain GREEN
- **Low-distraction environment**: Phone on airplane mode, notifications blocked, browser closed during learning blocks
- **Active recall over re-reading**: Flashcards or free recall must constitute ≥60% of study time (not passive review)

### 3.2 Cognitive Load Management

**Primary References**: *The Art of Learning* (Waitzkin), *Ultralearning* (Young)

**Hard Rules**:
- **Working memory limits**: Single cognitive task should require no more than 3–4 chunks of information simultaneously
- **Chunk formation**: New information must be connected to existing schema within 24h or it is lost
- **Cognitive load scoring**: Rate perceived difficulty 1–10 before each learning session. See threshold table.
- **Desirable difficulty**:适度 трудность (optimal struggle zone) — learning should feel effortful but solvable within the session
- **Emotional regulation during learning**: Anxiety >7/10 during learning sessions degrades hippocampus-dependent encoding — session should be aborted

**Cognitive Load Threshold Table**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Perceived difficulty (1–10) | 4–6 | 7–8 | 9–10 |
| Cognitive load score (1–10) | 1–5 | 6–7 | 8–10 |
| Session completion rate | ≥80% | 60–79% | <60% |
| Recovery debt (accumulated missed sleep) | 0–3h | 4–7h | >7h |

### 3.3 Learning Velocity Metrics

**Hard Rules**:
- **Weekly learning progress review**: Every Sunday, 30min minimum
- **Ultralearning project cycles**: 10-week sprints. Between cycles: 1 week of integration/downtime (no new content)
- **Skill acquisition tracking**: Each new competency must have a measurable output within 20h of initial study
- **Mastery criteria**: Ability to teach the concept to a novice without notes within 4 weeks of initial exposure

## 4. Beth HALT Protocol — Decision Tree

> **HALT** = Hungry, Angry, Lonely, Tired — but for Jerry Bio, the operative HALT is:
> **H**ealth, **A**daptation capacity, **L**earning threshold, **T**oxic load

### 4.1 Decision Tree: LD03 Signal Degradation

```
STEP 1: Any LD03 ORANGE signal detected (sleep <6h, HRV 60–64ms, exercise <2×/week)

  → Jerry Bio issues ORANGE ADVISORY to all Jerry areas
  → Recovery protocol MANDATORY (see Section 6)
  → Business expansion decisions PAUSE until ORANGE clears
  → Log event with timestamp in Jerry Bio scorecard

STEP 2: LD03 ORANGE persists >72h OR first RED signal appears

  → Jerry Bio issues HARD FREEZE to LD01 (Business Pulse)
  → Beth HALT veto is TRIGGERED
  → Beth receives automated notification: "LD03 substrate compromised — business expansion suspended"
  → Freeze remains until: 2 consecutive GREEN days on ALL LD03 metrics

STEP 3: LD03 RED on any single metric (sleep <5h, HRV <45ms, exercise 0×/week)

  → Jerry Bio issues FULL STOP to ALL Jerry areas
  → Beth HALT veto ENFORCED — no Jerry area may initiate new commitments
  → Recovery protocol ESCALATED (see Section 6.2)
  → Full activity log submitted to Beth within 24h
  → Clearance from Jerry Bio required before ANY area resumes normal operations
```

### 4.2 Decision Tree: LD04 Follows LD03

```
STEP 4: LD03 ORANGE + LD04 simultaneously ORANGE

  → LD04 cognitive load is now degraded AND LD03 substrate is degraded
  → Compound risk: decision quality AND physiological resilience both compromised
  → Beth HALT veto AUTOMATIC — this is the highest-probability failure mode
  → All non-essential learning SUSPENDED
  → Essential only: maintenance of existing skills (no new acquisition)

STEP 5: LD03 clears but LD04 remains ORANGE/RED

  → LD03 recovery takes priority (sleep first)
  → LD04 may not recover without LD03 substrate restoration
  → Business decisions made under LD04 ORANGE carry 2× quality discount — must be flagged to Beth

STEP 6: LD04 RED alone (cognitive load >9/10 without LD03 ORANGE)

  → RARE case — usually LD03 precedes
  → Issue HARD FREEZE to learning acquisition
  → Business decisions from the affected area require second-review by adjacent Jerry area
  → Identify source: sleep debt? emotional overload? information density?
```

## 5. Founder Load Ceilings

### 5.1 Capacity Model

Jerry Bio operates on a **finite resource pool** model. Business expansion consumes cognitive and vitality resources. The Founder Load Ceiling defines how much expansion the current substrate can support without breaching LD03/LD04 thresholds.

**Resource Pool**:
```
Total Daily Capacity = f(sleep_quality, HRV_trend, cognitive_load_history, exercise_consistency)
```

### 5.2 Ceiling Rules

- **New commitment threshold**: Any new business commitment >2h/day requires Jerry Bio clearance
- **Weekly commitment ceiling**: >45h/week of cognitive business work without adequate recovery = automatic ORANGE review
- **Simultaneous active projects**: Maximum 3 concurrent LD01 projects in execution phase (not planning)
- **High-cognitive-load activities** (negotiations, complex problem-solving, strategic planning):
  - Limited to 3×/week in GREEN state
  - Limited to 1×/week in ORANGE state
  - Prohibited in RED state
- **Meeting density ceiling**: >5h/day of synchronous cognitive work triggers automatic self-audit

### 5.3 Ceiling Enforcement

```
IF proposed activity + current load > Founder Load Ceiling
  → Jerry Bio issues DENIAL with specific ceiling reason
  → Proposal must be deferred OR distributed to another Jerry area OR
  → Jerry Bio must approve recovery period before activity may proceed
```

## 6. Recovery Policies

### 6.1 ORANGE Recovery Protocol (Mandatory)

When any single LD03 or LD04 metric enters ORANGE:

**Immediate actions (within 24h)**:
1. Identify primary stressor — sleep debt, HRV decline, cognitive overload, or exercise gap
2. Remove one non-essential commitment from schedule (not defer — remove)
3. Extend sleep window by 30min minimum
4. Mandatory 20min morning sunlight exposure (regulates circadian HRV)
5. Reduce Zone 2 training load by 50% (maintain movement, reduce intensity)
6. Log all 6 LD03 + 4 LD04 metrics in weekly scorecard

**Duration**: Minimum 72h of protocol adherence before ORANGE clears. Cannot clear on metrics alone — behavioral compliance required.

### 6.2 RED Recovery Protocol (Escalated)

When any metric enters RED:

**Immediate actions (within 12h)**:
1. Hard freeze on all new commitments across all Jerry areas
2. Sleep extension to 8–9h for minimum 3 consecutive nights
3. Eliminate ALL high-cognitive-load activities for minimum 72h
4. Zone 2 and mobility only — no Zone 5, no heavy resistance training
5. Submit activity log to Beth with root cause analysis
6. No business travel, negotiations, or strategic planning sessions
7. Follow-up assessment at 72h — if RED persists, escalate to Beth for external support review

**Prevents**: RED → chronic LD03/LD04 failure → Beth full shutdown of Jerry operations

### 6.3 ORANGE → GREEN Clearance Criteria

All three must be met:
1. Target metric back to GREEN for minimum 48h
2. All other LD03/LD04 metrics also GREEN
3. No new ORANGE signals introduced during recovery period

## 7. Cognitive Bandwidth Gates

### 7.1 Learning → Business Expansion Feedback

LD04 learning velocity directly constrains LD01 business expansion velocity. This is not metaphorical — it is neurological.

**Mechanism**:
- Working memory capacity is finite and degrades under LD03 ORANGE
- New business domains require new schema formation (LD04)
- If LD04 learning velocity is declining, new business domains will be absorbed at lower quality
- Cognitive bandwidth gates prevent LD01 from expanding faster than LD04 can absorb new domains

### 7.2 Bandwidth Gate Thresholds

| LD04 State | New Domain Learning | Business Expansion Gate |
|------------|--------------------|------------------------|
| GREEN (velocity stable/rising) | Allowed — 1 new domain per 10-week cycle | LD01 may add 1 new area |
| ORANGE (velocity declining) | Suspended — maintenance only | LD01 expansion PAUSED |
| RED (velocity negative) | Prohibited | LD01 CONTRACTION recommended |

### 7.3 Gate Enforcement Rule

```
IF LD04 weekly learning velocity is declining for 3+ consecutive weeks
  → Jerry Bio issues bandwidth warning to LD01
  → LD01 must either:
     a) Reduce cognitive business load to restore learning capacity, OR
     b) Demonstrate measurable LD04 improvement within 4 weeks
  → IF neither → Beth HALT veto applies to LD01 expansion
```

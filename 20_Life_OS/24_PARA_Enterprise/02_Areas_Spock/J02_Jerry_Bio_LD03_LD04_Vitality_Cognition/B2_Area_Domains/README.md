# B2 — Jerry Bio: 8 Biological Stack Domains
## Overview: The Biological Stack is CONSTRAINTS, Not Growth Drivers

> **Critical distinction**: The 8 biological domains are NOT business development domains.
> They are HARD SAFETY CONSTRAINTS. Their job is to PROTECT the substrate — not to expand it.
> A domain is successful when it stays GREEN. A domain is failing when it signals ORANGE or RED.

---

### LD03 — Vitality Stack (6 domains)

| # | Domain | Primary Source | What It Protects |

| 1 | **Sleep/Recovery** | Why We Sleep (Walker) | Cognitive function, memory consolidation, emotional regulation |

## 3. CONSTRAINT vs GROWTH Logic

### 3.1 Business Domains (LD01 style)

```
Business domain success = MORE, FASTER, BIGGER
  → Growth is the goal
  → ORANGE = behind schedule → accelerate
  → GREEN = on track → expand
```

### 3.2 Biological Domains (J02 style)

```
Biological domain success = SAME, STABLE, PROTECTED
  → Stability is the goal
  → ORANGE = deterioration → intervene
  → GREEN = protected → maintain
  → RED = failure → STOP everything
```

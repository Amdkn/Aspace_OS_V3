# W01–W04: Winter/Foundational

## Quarter Focus

**Purpose**: Establish winter baseline, account for seasonal biology, set the foundation for the year.

**Key Insight**: Winter biology is the hardest season for J02. HRV is typically 10–15% lower than summer, vitamin D is at seasonal low, and the absence of natural light makes sleep and mood management harder. This is not the quarter for optimization — it is the quarter for holding the line.

---

### Focus

```

```

### Key Actions

```
□ Pull prior year annual biological audit (if exists)
□ Establish W01 baseline metrics (all 8 domains measured)

□ Implement winter protocol adjustments:
```

## 6. Circular Economy Hierarchy

From most to least preferred:

1. **Redesign** — eliminate waste at design stage
2. **Reuse** — use things again for same purpose
3. **Repair** — maintain and fix rather than replace
4. **Refurbish** — restore to like-new condition
5. **Remanufacture** — use parts in new product
6. **Repurpose** — use materials for different function
7. **Recycle** — break down to raw materials (last resort)
8. **Recover** — extract energy (incineration)
9. **Dispose** — landfill (failure state)

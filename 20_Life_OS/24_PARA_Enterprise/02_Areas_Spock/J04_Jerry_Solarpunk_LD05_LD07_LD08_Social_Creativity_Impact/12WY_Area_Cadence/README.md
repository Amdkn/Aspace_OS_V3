## 1. Cadence Philosophy

J04's 12WY cadence is about **SEASONAL CONTRIBUTION RHYTHM** — not arbitrary calendar-fixing, but aligning relational seasons, creativity cycles, and annual MUSE review to the natural arc of the year.

The contribution stack has its own seasonality:
- **Winter (W01-W04)**: Deep relational work — network audits, trust velocity assessment, quality over quantity
- **Spring (W05-W08)**: Creativity activation — experiential capital deployment, first MUSE projects launched
- **Summer (implied)**: Scaling — building on spring momentum
- **Autumn (W09-W12)**: Contribution harvest — MUSE review, public benefit assessment, doctrine annual check

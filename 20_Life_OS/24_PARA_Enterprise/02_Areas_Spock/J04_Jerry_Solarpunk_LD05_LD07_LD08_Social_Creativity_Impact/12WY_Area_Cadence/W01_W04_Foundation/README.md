## 1. Phase Identity

W01-W04 is the **Foundation Phase** — winter relational deep work. This is when Jerry builds the trust infrastructure that will power the rest of the year. Quality over quantity. Depth over breadth.

### Core Focus

- Network audit: who are our real allies?
- Trust velocity baseline: how fast do we build trust?
- Quality assessment: which relationships are assets vs liabilities?
- Relational deposit-making: giver-first before asking

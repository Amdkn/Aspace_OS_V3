## 2. Naval/Anchoring Philosophy Spine

*Philosophical foundation from which all financial decisions are made*

### The 4 Outputs (Naval Ravikant)

All time and energy must produce one of:
1. **Money** — compounding knowledge + equity
2. **Health** — physical + mental
3. **Relationships** — deep, not wide
4. **Freedom** — no dependent commitments

If any business activity does not advance at least one of these four, reject it.

### The Sovereignty Questions (apply to every financial decision)

1. Is this decision reversible? (If yes, move fast. If no, slow down and weigh heavily.)
2. Does this reduce my dependence on any single income stream?
3. Does this increase or decrease my optionality in 12 months?
4. Am I deciding from security (GREEN zone) or scarcity (RED/ORANGE zone)?
5. Is this a wealth-building decision or an identity-signaling decision?

> Being rich and being wealthy are different things. Wealth is income you don't spend. Rich is spending freely. Build wealth through invisible work and compound patience.

### The Asset Rule (Rich Dad Poor Dad)

## 3. Independence Threshold Framework

*The numbers that gate expansion — these are non-negotiable*

### The 4% Rule Baseline

```
Target Passive Income = Total Invested Assets × 0.04
```

This assumes a 4% withdrawal rate. Conservative during accumulation phase. Do not count home equity.

### The Coverage Ratio

```
Coverage Ratio = Monthly Passive Income / Monthly Essential Burn
```

Essential burn = non-negotiable personal + business floor costs. Excludes growth investments.

| Coverage Ratio | Status | Expansion Permission |
|----------------|--------|---------------------|
| < 60% | RED | NO expansion. Defend only. |
| 60–79% | ORANGE | Minimal. Only if increases coverage within 90 days. |
| 80–99% | YELLOW | Cautious. Max 20% surplus reinvest. |
| 100%+ | GREEN | Normal expansion permitted. |

### The Runway Floor

```
Runway = Cash on Hand / Monthly Net Burn
```

Do not count committed future revenue until received.

| Runway | Status | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| < 3 months | RED | Full stop. No new commitments. Preserve cash. |
| 3–5 months | ORANGE | Survive mode. Revenue focus only. |
| 6–11 months | YELLOW | Cautious growth. One commitment at a time. |
| 12+ months | GREEN | Normal operations. |

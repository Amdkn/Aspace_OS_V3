# W01-W04 — Foundation Season
## Tax + Planning: Prior Year Review, Tax Strategy, Annual Setup

---

## Season Identity

W01-W04 is the **reset and plan** season. The prior year closes, taxes get filed, and the new year gets established with clear financial targets. This is the foundation upon which Q2-Q4 growth is built.

---

## W01 — Year Close + Prior Year Analysis

### Focus
- Close out prior year books
- Confirm final income, expenses, taxes paid
- Calculate prior year coverage ratio
- Assess prior year KRs (what worked, what didn't)

### Key Actions

```
□ Pull final bank statements for December
□ Calculate final runway months at year end
□ Confirm coverage ratio for prior year
□ Calculate total taxes paid vs. taxes owed
□ Assess W01-W52 performance: what worked?
□ Draft prior year financial summary
```

### Deliverable: Prior Year Close Report

```
Prior Year Financial Summary:
- Total Income: $____
- Total Expenses: $____
- Net Savings: $____
- Savings Rate: ___%
- End-of-Year Runway: ___ months
- End-of-Year Coverage: ___%
- Taxes Paid: $____
- Effective Tax Rate: ___%
```

## W02 — Tax Filing + Strategy Initiation

### Focus
- File prior year taxes (or extension)
- Establish current year tax projection
- Calculate quarterly estimated tax requirements
- Begin tax optimization planning

### Key Actions

```
□ Finalize and file prior year return (or file extension)
□ Set up quarterly estimated tax schedule
□ Calculate tax reserve requirement (25% of revenue)
□ Review prior year tax for optimization opportunities
□ Confirm entity structure still optimal
□ Schedule CPA consultation for current year
```

### Tax Projection Template

```
- Projected Expenses: $____
- Projected Net: $____
- Estimated Tax (___%): $____
- Quarterly Estimate: $____ per quarter
- Tax Reserve Target: $____ (in quarterly reserve)
```

## W03 — Budget Establishment + Goal Setting

### Focus
- Establish annual budget (essential burn baseline)
- Set 12-month financial targets
- Assign monthly KR targets
- Confirm 6-account allocation percentages

### Key Actions

```
□ Confirm essential burn (monthly baseline)
□ Set monthly savings target (20%+ of income)
□ Establish runway goal (12 months target)
□ Set coverage ratio target (>80% annual average)
□ Confirm all 6 accounts funded automatically
□ Document annual financial goals (1-year targets)
```

## W01-W04 Success Criteria

| Metric | Target | Measure |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Tax Filed | By Feb 1 (or extension) | Filing confirmation |
| Tax Reserve | 25% of revenue in reserve | Account balance |
| Q1 Coverage | > 80% OR at least improving | Monthly tracking |
| Budget Established | All accounts automated | Confirmation check |
| Goals Set | 1-year targets documented | Goals document |

---

## Common Failure Modes

- **Filing late**: Creates stress, penalties, catch-up pressure
- **Under-funding tax reserve**: Creates cash flow shock at filing
- **Skipping goal-setting**: Drives reactive, not proactive, management
- **Not reviewing insurance**: Coverage gaps discovered at worst time

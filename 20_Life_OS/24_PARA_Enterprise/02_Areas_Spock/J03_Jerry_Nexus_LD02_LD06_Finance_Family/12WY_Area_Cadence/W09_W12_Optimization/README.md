# W09-W12 — Optimization Season
## Year-End + Succession: Runway Confirmation, Insurance Renewal, Succession Check

---

## Season Identity

W09-W12 is the **harvest and secure** season. Year-end brings tax optimization opportunities, insurance renewals, succession planning updates, and the final check of whether the annual financial targets will be met. It's the culmination of the 12WY.

---

## W09 — Tax-Loss Harvesting + Year-End Planning

### Focus
- Tax-loss harvesting review (final window before year-end)
- Q3 close and Q4 projection
- Final tax optimization moves
- Charitable giving (year-end tax advantage)

### Key Actions

```
□ Review portfolio for tax-loss harvesting opportunities
□ Harvest losses before Dec 31 cutoff
□ Confirm charitable giving completed for tax year
□ Review Q3 actuals vs. budget
□ Project full-year numbers
□ Confirm final tax reserve adequate
```

### Tax-Loss Harvesting Rules

```
Rule 1: Only harvest if washing sale rules satisfied (30-day window)
Rule 2: Document all harvests for audit trail
Rule 3: Reinvest in similar (not identical) asset to maintain exposure
Rule 4: Don't harvest just for tax reasons — must make investment sense
```

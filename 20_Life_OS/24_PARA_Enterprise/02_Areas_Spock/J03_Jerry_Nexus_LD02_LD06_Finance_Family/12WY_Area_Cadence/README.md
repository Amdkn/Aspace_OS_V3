# 12WY — Jerry Nexus Area Cadence
## Annual Financial Rhythm: Tax, Insurance, Investment, Succession

---

## Purpose

The 12WY cadence transforms J03 from reactive financial management into **structured financial rhythm**. Just as the 12-week year creates urgency and focus in operations, the J03 12WY creates a predictable annual cycle for financial architecture reviews.

---

## The Four Seasons of J03

| Season | Weeks | Focus | Key Events |
|--------|-------|-------|------------|
| W01-W04 | Foundation | Tax + Planning | Prior year review, tax filing, Q1 projection |
| W05-W08 | Scaling | Growth + Deployment | Capital deployment, investment review, mid-year check |
| W09-W12 | Optimization | Year-End + Succession | Runway confirmation, insurance renewal, succession check |

---

## Quarterly Deep Dives

### Q1 (W01-W04): Foundation Season
- Prior year tax filing
- Current year tax projection
- Budget establishment
- Insurance renewal review (health)
- Establish financial goals for year

### Q2 (W05-W08): Scaling Season
- Mid-year coverage ratio check
- Capital deployment review (H1 deployment status)
- TIER 2 rebalancing check
- Mid-year tax estimate adjustment
- Hard thing streak check

### Q3 (W09-W12): Optimization Season
- Year-end tax planning
- Tax-loss harvesting review
- Insurance renewal (life, property)
- Succession planning review
- Year-end financial snapshot
- Next year projection and goal setting

---

## Weekly Rhythms

### Weekly Gate Check (Every Sunday, 15 min)

```
1. Runway status check (current months)
2. Coverage ratio check (current %)
3. Family load score (self-assessment)
4. Any ORANGE or RED flags?
5. If GREEN+: proceed with planned activities
6. If ORANGE/RED: escalation protocol
```

### Monthly Scorecard Review (1st Sunday, 1 hour)

1. Pull actuals for all KRs
2. Update scorecard
3. Identify any KRs in ORANGE or RED
4. Draft recovery plan if needed
5. Brief A0 on status

### Quarterly Deep Dive (Quarter start, 3 hours)

1. Full financial architecture review
2. TIER 1-4 status check
3. Entity structure review
4. Insurance coverage audit
5. Advisor consultation (CPA, financial advisor)

## Cross-Season Logic

```
W01-W04 (Foundation)
    ↓
    Creates: Tax strategy, annual budget, insurance renewals
    Feeds into: W05-W08 capital deployment decisions
    ↓
W05-W08 (Scaling)
    ↓
    Creates: Mid-year correction, deployment status, investment review
    Feeds into: W09-W12 optimization decisions
    ↓
W09-W12 (Optimization)
    ↓
    Creates: Year-end position, next year plan, succession update
    Feeds back into: Next W01-W04 foundation season
```

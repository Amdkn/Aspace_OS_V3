## Purpose

The Warp Core is the **operational heartbeat** of J03. It contains the Lead/Lag metrics that tell you whether the financial architecture is accelerating or decelerating, and the Artifact proofs that document the decisions made.

## Area-Level Lead/Lag Framework

### Lead Metrics (Predictive — Moving the needle)

| Lead Metric | Definition | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|-------------|------------|-------|--------|-----|
| Savings Rate | % of income saved/invested | > 20% | 10-20% | < 10% |
| Capital Deployment | % of Reinvestment Pool deployed | 15% on schedule | 10-14% | < 10% |
| Risk Coverage | % of key risks mitigated | 100% | 80-99% | < 80% |
| Tax Optimization | Tax strategy completion | 100% | 80-99% | < 80% |

### Lag Metrics (Outcome — The scoreboard)

| Lag Metric | Definition | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|------------|------------|-------|--------|-----|
| Runway Months | Cash / Monthly Burn | > 12 | 6-12 | < 6 |
| Coverage Ratio | Passive Income / Essential Burn | > 100% | 80-100% | < 80% |
| Passive Income Ratio | Passive Income / Total Income | > 50% | 25-50% | < 25% |
| Family Load Score | 10 - (Work Hrs / Ideal Hrs) × 10 | > 8 | 6-8 | < 6 |

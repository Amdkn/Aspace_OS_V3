# B2-D07 — Insurance Protection
## Deductible Reserves + Coverage Adequacy Review

---

## Domain Identity

Insurance Protection is the **final risk layer** — ensuring that catastrophic events (health crisis, death, disability, liability) don't collapse the runway. It manages insurance coverage review and deductible reserve funding.

Without insurance, a single catastrophic event ends the journey. With it, the journey continues.

## Core Insurance Categories

### Health Insurance
- Major medical with HSA-compatible plan
- Deductible reserve in PROTECT account (D01)
- Term life insurance (if family dependents)

### Disability Insurance
- Income replacement if unable to work
- Own-occupation definition preferred
- 60-70% income replacement target

### Liability Insurance
- Umbrella policy (1-2M minimum)
- Auto liability minimums
- Homeowner liability
- Professional liability (E&O)

### Property Insurance
- Homeowner/renter
- Business property
- Key equipment replacement

---

## The Deductible Reserve Rule

```
PROTECT Account = Largest Deductible (typically $5,000-$10,000)
```

This account is funded monthly until fully capitalized. It ensures a deductible payment doesn't breach the runway.

## The Four Tiers

### TIER 1 — Safety Core
- 6 months runway in cash
- NOT investment capital
- Psychological safety, not yield

### TIER 2 — Compound Engine
- Broad index funds (VTI/VTWAX style)
- 10-20 year patient compounding
- 20% of all income flows here automatically
- The "boring but right" approach

### TIER 3 — Business Equity
- AaaS, Solaris, Nexus, Orbiter stakes
- Non-linear upside potential
- 15% of income via Reinvestment Pool
- Only deploy when TIER 1 is GREEN

### TIER 4 — Opportunistic
- Real estate, ventures, alternatives
- Asymmetric bets only
- Only when all gates are SURGE

---

## Non-Negotiable Sequence

1. Build TIER 1 to GREEN before TIER 3
2. Build TIER 2 to GREEN before TIER 4
3. Never skip tiers

## The 4% Rule

```
Target Portfolio = Annual Expenses × 25
(4% safe withdrawal rate)

OR for safety margin:
Target Portfolio = Annual Expenses × 33
(3% withdrawal rate)
```

## Investment Principles

1. **Low cost**: Index funds over actively managed (expense ratios matter)
2. **Diversified**: No single asset > 20% of portfolio
3. **Automatic**: All contributions automated, no manual timing
4. **Patient**: 10-20 year horizon, ignore short-term noise
5. **Documented**: All positions logged, cost basis tracked

# B2-D06 — Succession Planning
## Entity Structures + Estate Basics + Business Continuity

---

## Domain Identity

Succession Planning is the **preservation layer** — ensuring accumulated capital and business equity survives across generations and transitions. It manages entity structures, estate documents, and business continuity plans.

Without succession planning, wealth is fragile. With it, wealth is transferable.

## Core Components

### 1. Entity Structure Review

| Entity Type | Use Case | Succession Advantage |
|-------------|----------|---------------------|
| S-Corp | Self-employment income | Pass-through taxation, reasonable salary |
| LLC | Business equity protection | Liability shield, flexible ownership |
| C-Corp | Investment holding | Tax-advantaged retention for reinvestment |

### 2. Estate Documentation

- **Will**: Specifies asset distribution
- **Healthcare Directive**: Medical decision-making authority
- **Power of Attorney**: Financial decision-making if incapacitated
- **Beneficiary Designations**: Retirement accounts, life insurance

### 3. Business Continuity

- Key person dependencies identified
- Client relationship documentation
- Process documentation for operations
- Succession candidates identified

---

## Jerry's Stewardship Approach

- **Annually**: Full estate document review with attorney
- **Quarterly**: Business continuity check-in
- **Ongoing**: Ensure all accounts have beneficiary designations
- **Trigger**: Birth of child, marriage, major asset purchase, business change

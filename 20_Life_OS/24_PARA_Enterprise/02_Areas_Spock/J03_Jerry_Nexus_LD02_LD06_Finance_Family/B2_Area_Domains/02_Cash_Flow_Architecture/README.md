# B2-D02 — Cash Flow Architecture
## Income/Expense Rhythm + Automated Banking

---

## Domain Identity

Cash Flow Architecture is the **rhythm layer** — the income/expense cadence that determines whether capital builds or depletes. It operationalizes the 6-account structure and tracks the coverage ratio (passive income / expenses).

Without cash flow architecture, income is chaotic. With it, money flows on rails.

## Core Doctrine

**The Cash Flow Rule**: Income is not victory. Cash flow sustainability is victory.

### Coverage Ratio Calculation

```
Coverage Ratio = Monthly Passive Income / Monthly Essential Burn
```

| Coverage | Status | Expansion Permission |
|----------|--------|----------------------|
| < 60% | RED | NO expansion. Defend only. |
| 60–79% | ORANGE | Minimal. Only if increases coverage within 90 days. |
| 80–99% | YELLOW | Cautious. Max 20% surplus reinvest. |
| 100%+ | GREEN | Normal expansion. |

---

## Essential Burn Components

Track these as the true cost of sovereignty:
- Housing (not mortgage > 28% gross income)
- Healthcare (marketplace + deductible reserve)
- Basic mobility (vehicle + insurance)
- Food sovereignty (home + quality protein)
- A0 presence protection (no-work zones, family time)

---

## Jerry's Stewardship Approach

- **Primary**: Track coverage ratio monthly, enforce automation mandates
- **Weekly**: Verify all 6 accounts funded on schedule
- **Monthly**: Full cash flow review — actual vs. budget, coverage trajectory
- **Trigger**: Coverage ratio below 80% → expense audit and reduction protocol

## 6-Account Funding Logic

```
Income Received
       ↓
BUFFER (1 month essential) ← automated first
       ↓
REINVEST (15%) ← never touches checking
       ↓
INDEPENDENCE (20% of surplus) ← flows to index funds
       ↓
PROTECT (deductible reserve) ← until funded
       ↓
PLAY (5%) ← presence fund, non-negotiable
       ↓
GIVE (1%) ← charitable reserve
```

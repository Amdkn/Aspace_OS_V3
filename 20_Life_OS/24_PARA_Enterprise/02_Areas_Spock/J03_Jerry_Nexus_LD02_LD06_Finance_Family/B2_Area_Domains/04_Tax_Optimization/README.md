## Core Doctrine

**The Tax Rule**: Tax optimization is not tax evasion. Pay the minimum legal amount, not the maximum.

### Legal Optimization Strategies

1. **Entity Structure**: S-Corp, LLC, or C-Corp election to optimize self-employment tax
2. **Retirement Contributions**: Max SIMPLE IRA, Solo 401(k), or SEP-IRA
3. **Health Insurance Deduction**: Self-employed health insurance deduction
4. **Home Office Deduction**: If applicable, legitimate business use
5. **Business Expenses**: Track and deduct all legitimate business expenses
6. **Tax-Loss Harvesting**: Realize losses to offset gains in investment accounts

---

## Tax Reserve Policy

**Rule**: 25% of all revenue auto-streams to tax reserve quarterly.

```
Tax Reserve = Revenue × 0.25
```

This ensures no tax surprise at filing time. The reserve grows until April 15, then resets.

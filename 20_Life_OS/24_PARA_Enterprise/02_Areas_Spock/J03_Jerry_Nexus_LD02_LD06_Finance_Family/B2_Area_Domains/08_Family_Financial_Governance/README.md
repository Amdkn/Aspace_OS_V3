## The Family Financial OS

### What It Means

A documented system that:
1. Ensures family financial needs are met before business needs
2. Protects presence windows as non-negotiable
3. Detects when financial anxiety bleeds into family time
4. Creates accountability for financial decisions affecting family

### Core Documents

- **Family Financial Baseline**: What the family needs, documented annually
- **Presence Calendar**: Protected family time, blocked on calendar
- **Resistance Protocol**: How to detect when busyness masquerades as family focus
- **Decision Escalation**: What requires A0, what Jerry manages

## Resistance Detection Checklist

| Resistance Signal | Counter-Protocol |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| "I'll just check one thing" before dinner | Devices in another room. Physical boundary. |
| "I need to finish this first" | Done is never finished. Presence window is sacred. |
| Anxiety about work while with family | Journal the worry. 15-minute worry time, then drop it. |
| Reaching for phone during conversation | Phone face-down, out of reach. |
| "Tomorrow I'll be fully present" | Today is the only day available. |

# B2 — J03 Jerry Nexus Area Domains

---

## Domain Overview

J03 operates 8 financial architecture domains that form the **stability membrane** surrounding all expansion, investment, and reinvestment decisions within the Jerry Nexus. Where J01 drives revenue and J04 drives growth, J03 exists to ensure the runway survives long enough for those initiatives to compound.

These are **constraint domains** — they define the financial operating system that enables J01, J02, and J04 to operate. They say "no" to urgent revenue when stability is at risk.

## Core Doctrine

**The Capital Rule**: Capital is protection, not investment. Emergency reserve is NOT investment capital — it is psychological safety, not yield.

### The 6-Account Structure

| Account | Purpose | Allocation |
|---------|---------|------------|
| BUFFER | 1 month essential burn | Automatic on payday |
| REINVEST | Business growth allocation | 15% of all income |
| INDEPENDENCE | Long-term index fund compound | 20% of surplus |
| PROTECT | Insurance deductible reserve | $X monthly until funded |
| PLAY | A0 presence fund | 5% of income |
| GIVE | Charitable/gift reserve | 1% of income |

**Automation Mandate**: All allocations execute automatically on paydays. The Reinvestment Pool and Wealth Builder never touch checking — they flow directly to investment vehicles.

---

## Jerry's Stewardship Approach

- **Primary**: Ensure all 6 accounts are funded on schedule, every payday
- **Weekly**: Runway calculation (cash / monthly burn)
- **Monthly**: Full capital review — reserve adequacy, automation health
- **Trigger**: Any gate hitting ORANGE → immediate capital review

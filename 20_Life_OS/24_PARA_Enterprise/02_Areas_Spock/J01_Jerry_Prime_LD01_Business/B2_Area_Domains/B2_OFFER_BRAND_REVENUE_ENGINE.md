# B2 Offer Brand Revenue Engine - J01_Jerry_Prime_LD01_Business

## Purpose

The B2 council converts B1 direction into an offer that can sell, deliver, and compound as a brand.

## Domain Responsibilities

| Domain | Contribution |
|---|---|
| Growth | ICP, message, category, channel, brand wedge. |
| Sales | qualification, objections, buying path, close evidence. |
| Product | promise-to-feature fit and demo proof. |
| Ops | delivery SOP and capacity limits. |
| IT | runtime, data, access, automation, integration boundaries. |
| Finance | price, cost, margin, payment, burn-rate. |
| People | owner map, training, load, handoff. |
| Legal | claims, privacy, IP, terms, compliance. |

## Required Packets

1. Offer packet: ICP, pain, dream outcome, time delay, effort removed, risk reversal, proof, price logic.
2. Brand packet: category, enemy, signature mechanism, first channel, story asset.
3. Revenue packet: price hypothesis, cost floor, margin target, payment path, upsell/cross-sell hypothesis.
4. Delivery packet: SOP, owner, support path, B3 execution boundary.

## Council Cadence

B2 reviews this engine before any project moves beyond PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE. If a domain cannot provide proof, it marks the missing proof and routes the blocker to B1 or the relevant B3 squad.

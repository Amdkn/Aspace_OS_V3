# E-Myth Franchise Prototype

## Doctrine

Every LD01 business system must be designed as if it could be handed to a trained operator, not only understood by its creator.

## Required Registers

| Register | Owner | Proof |
|---|---|---|
| Role map | B1 Jerry / B1 Summer | Named owner for every recurring function. |
| SOP map | B2 Ops | Delivery steps, quality gates, exception paths. |
| Training map | B2 People | What a new operator must know before touching execution. |
| Metrics map | B2 Finance + B2 Ops | Lead and lag indicators. |
| Exception map | B2 Legal + B2 IT | What must stop, escalate, or enter DLQ. |

## Franchise Test

A project is not a franchise prototype until another agent can receive a B3 JTBD packet, execute the work, produce proof, and hand it back without asking A0 to reinterpret the business.

## Anti-Patterns

- Founder memory is the process.
- Product artifact exists but no delivery SOP exists.
- Sales promise changes delivery manually each time.
- Finance cannot price or forecast the offer.
- People cannot onboard a replacement operator.

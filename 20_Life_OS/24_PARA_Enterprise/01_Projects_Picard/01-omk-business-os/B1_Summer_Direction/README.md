## ICP Variant Determination Rules

### Mode = Solaris (Visual-First) when:
- Primary OMK deliverable is brand/visual identity systems
- OMK client decides on portfolio/aesthetics
- CAC > $500, ASP > $15K, sales cycle < 30 days
- OMK client profile: design-first, speed-driven, brand-conscious

### Mode = Nexus (Data-First / Expert Knowledge) when:
- Primary OMK deliverable is business systems/operational expertise
- OMK client decides on credentials/case studies/systems evidence
- CAC < $500, ASP > $25K, sales cycle > 45 days
- OMK client profile: eval-intensive, evidence-driven, operational excellence oriented

### Mode = Orbiter (Field-First / Logistics) when:
- Primary OMK deliverable is execution/project management/operations
- OMK client decides on reliability/track record
- ASP > $50K, contract-heavy, relationship-driven
- OMK client profile: capacity-focused, long-term, franchise-oriented

**Override rule**: Jerry Prime can force mode shift with B1 approval when market signals diverge from revenue threshold.

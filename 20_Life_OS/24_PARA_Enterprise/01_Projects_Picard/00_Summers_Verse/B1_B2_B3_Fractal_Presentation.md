# Summer's Verse B1/B2/B3 Fractal

## B1 - Summer Direction

Summer is the project CEO. B1 defines why the project exists, where it is going, what must be true after each 12WY cycle, and what cannot be sacrificed.

Required horizons:

| Horizon | Question |
|---|---|
| 1 year | What must this project become within 4 cycles of 12WY? |
| 3 years | What strategic capability or MUSE does this become? |
| 10 years | What durable company asset, franchise, or civilization function does it serve? |

## B2 - Business Domains

B2 managers own Rocks and tactic specifications. They translate B1 vision into bounded outcomes.

They manage:

- Rock definition.
- Definition of Done.
- Tactical specification.
- Dependencies and gates.
- Assignment to B3.

## B3 - Warp Core Execution

B3 squads execute only. They do not rewrite the strategy, rename the Rock, or redefine the project.

They produce:

- Lead measure logs.
- Lag measure logs.
- Artifact proof.
- Blocker notes.
- Completion evidence.

## Handoff Rule

```text
B1 decides direction.
B2 defines Rocks and specs.
B3 executes Warp Core tactics.
Picard checks project structure.
Computer stores the decision.
```

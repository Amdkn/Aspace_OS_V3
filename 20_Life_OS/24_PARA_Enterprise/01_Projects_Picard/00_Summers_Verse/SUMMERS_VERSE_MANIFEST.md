# Summer's Verse Manifest - Template

## Purpose

`00_Summers_Verse` is the canonical Picard template for instantiating a finite Summer project under Jerry's perpetual Business Area.

The template repeats the Jerry fractal at project scale:

```text
Jerry Area (Spock, perpetual)
  B1_Area_Direction
  B2_Area_Domains
  B3_Area_Warp_Core

Summer Project (Picard, finite)
  B1_Summer_Direction
  B2_Business_Domains
  B3_Warp_Core_Execution
```

## Operating Rule

Summer projects reference Jerry doctrine; they do not re-derive it.

- B1 Summer calibrates the project vision, 12WY cycles, decision rights, handoff queue, and packet specs.
- B2 Business Domains instantiate the 8-domain Business Wheel and mark PASS, CONDITIONAL, or BLOCKED gates.
- B3 Warp Core executes JTBD packets and returns inspectable proof only.

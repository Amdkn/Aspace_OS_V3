# Market Validation Sprint - 00_Summers_Verse

## Purpose

This Summer Project must prove that the market cares before B2 and B3 overbuild.

## Sprint Contract

| Step | Owner | Proof |
|---|---|---|
| Define ICP | B1 + Growth | One named segment and exclusion list. |
| Define painful problem | B1 + Sales | Problem statement in customer language. |
| Draft irresistible offer | Growth + Sales + Finance | Offer packet with price hypothesis. |
| Run fast validation | B3 swarm | Outreach, interviews, landing page, demo, or waitlist evidence. |
| Capture objections | Sales + Product | Objection log mapped to offer/product changes. |
| Decide next status | B1 + B2 council | Continue, pivot, pause, or archive recommendation. |

## Validation Rule

A validation sprint is not a build sprint. The output is evidence of demand, willingness to engage, willingness to pay, or a clear reason to stop.

## Minimum Evidence

- ICP hypothesis.
- Offer hypothesis.
- 3 market contacts, interview notes, or equivalent signal.
- A decision record tied to the next 12WY cycle.

## ICP Variants — RILCOT Modes

### Nexus (Data-First / Member Knowledge) — PRIMARY
**Trigger**: Member value = knowledge exchange, expertise sharing, community data
**Profile**: Members who join for collective intelligence, peer learning, shared frameworks. Decision driven by member outcomes and knowledge depth.
**Mode markers**: Longer onboarding, evidence-driven value demonstration, thought leadership orientation.
**Primary books**: `Expert Secrets` (Canter), `The Consulting Bible` (Maister), `Group Genius` (Sawyer)

### Solaris (Visual-First / Member Experience) — SECONDARY
**Trigger**: Member value = branded experience, identity, sense of belonging
**Profile**: Members who join for community identity, curated experience, prestige. Decision driven by brand and visual presence.
**Mode markers**: High CAC, member retention driven by experience design, speed-first onboarding.
**Primary books**: `Building a StoryBrand` (Miller), `The Brand Flip` (Fisher)

### Orbiter (Field-First / Community Replication) — PHASE 3
**Trigger**: >$200K MRR, community franchise model
**Profile**: Members who join for proven model, local chapter activation, peer network.
**Mode markers**: Highest ASP, relationship-driven, contract-heavy chapters.
**Primary books**: `Built to Sell` (Warrilow), `The E-Myth Revisited` (Gerber)

## ICP Variant Determination Rules

### Mode = Solaris (Visual-First / Member Experience) when:
- Primary member value is brand identity and belonging
- Target member decides on community membership by brand impression
- CAC > $50, ASP > $500/year, onboarding < 7 days
- Member profile: experience-first, identity-driven, community-conscious

### Mode = Nexus (Data-First / Member Knowledge) when:
- Primary member value is knowledge exchange and peer learning
- Target member decides on community membership by credentials/outcomes
- CAC < $50, ASP > $1,000/year, onboarding > 14 days
- Member profile: eval-intensive, evidence-driven, thought leadership oriented

### Mode = Orbiter (Field-First / Community Replication) when:
- Primary member value is franchise model and local activation
- Target member decides on chapter/community by proven track record
- ASP > $5,000/year, contract-heavy, relationship-driven
- Member profile: capacity-focused, long-term, expansion-oriented

**Override rule**: Jerry Prime can force mode shift with B1 approval when member signals diverge from revenue threshold.

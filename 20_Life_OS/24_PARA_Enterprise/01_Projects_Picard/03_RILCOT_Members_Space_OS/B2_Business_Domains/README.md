# B2 Business Domains — 03 RILCOT Members Space & OS

All 8 domains below map to the J01 Area Standard. Each B2 manager holds their domain Rocks and cascade to B3 Marvel squads.

RILCOT Members Space OS is a **member community platform** — member value comes from collective knowledge, shared experience, and community belonging. Nexus mode is primary (member knowledge exchange). Solaris mode is secondary (member experience/branding).

## RILCOT-Specific Domain Emphasis

### Nexus Priority: G7 People / G3 Product / G2 Sales
For RILCOT as a member knowledge community, the Nexus primary mode emphasizes:
- **G7 People**: Peer facilitator network is the knowledge exchange engine
- **G3 Product**: Member experience IS the product — knowledge delivery systems
- **G2 Sales**: Member onboarding converts based on community fit and peer outcomes

### Solaris Priority: G1 Growth / G4 Ops / G3 Product
For RILCOT Solaris secondary mode:
- **G1 Growth**: Community brand equity drives member referral
- **G4 Ops**: Member experience platform powers the OS
- **G3 Product**: Curated member journey as branded experience

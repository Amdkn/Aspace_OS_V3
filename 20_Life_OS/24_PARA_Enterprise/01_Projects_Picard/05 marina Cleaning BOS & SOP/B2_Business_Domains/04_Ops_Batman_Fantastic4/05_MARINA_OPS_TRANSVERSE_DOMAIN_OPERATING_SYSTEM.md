# MARINA Ops Transverse Domain Operating System

## Purpose
Ops is the transverse gate for Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP. Its job is to turn the eight B2 domains into safe, visible, repeatable field delivery.

## Operating Thesis
Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP is field-first. Ops must turn cleaning delivery into SOPs, crew execution, quality checks, weather-aware scheduling, and client walkthroughs.

## Transverse Flow
1. People confirms crew roles, training, load, and accountability.
2. IT confirms scheduling, route, records, photos, and client communication tools.
3. Product confirms service packages, quality levels, and exclusions.
4. Growth confirms client promise, sales intake, and expectation boundaries.
5. Finance confirms quote, margin, supplies, labor cost, and payment timing.
6. Legal confirms insurance, safety, terms, and liability boundaries.
7. Ops converts all inputs into route plan, SOP, checklist, QA, and escalation.
8. B3 executes only after Ops issues the transverse readiness decision.

## Eight-Domain Ops Interface
| Domain | Ops Question | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| People | Who cleans, supervises, and checks quality? | Crew map and training note |
| IT | How is the job tracked? | Schedule, route, photo/record path |
| Ops | What procedure runs in the field? | SOP, checklist, escalation |
| Product | What service is sold? | Package, scope, exclusions |
| Growth | What did the client expect? | Intake and promise record |
| Finance | Is the job profitable? | Quote, labor/supply cost, payment |
| Legal | Is the job safe and covered? | Insurance/safety/terms check |
| Integration | What can break the job? | Risk and blocker log |

## Project Constraint
Field delivery is the product: safety, equipment, crew load, weather, QA, and client satisfaction must be operationally visible.

## Ops Metrics
- Job readiness.
- Crew capacity.
- SOP completion.
- QA pass rate.
- Client issue rate.
- Margin by job.

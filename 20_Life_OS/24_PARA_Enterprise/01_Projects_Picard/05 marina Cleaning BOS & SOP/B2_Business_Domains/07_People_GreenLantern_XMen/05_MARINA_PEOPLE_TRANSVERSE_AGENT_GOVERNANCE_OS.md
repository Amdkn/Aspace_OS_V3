# MARINA People Transverse Agent Governance OS

## Purpose
People is the transverse governance domain for all B2 owners and B3 agents in Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP. It assigns who thinks, who decides, who executes, who reviews, and who catches failures across the eight business domains.

## Operating Thesis
Marina needs People to make field crews, supervisors, QA owners, and client-facing roles visible before jobs execute.

## Agent Governance Layers
| Layer | Role | People Responsibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| B1 Summer Direction | Project command | Confirm the mission, priority, and active gates |
| B2 Domain Owners | Domain decision | Own domain judgment, constraints, and acceptance proof |
| B3 Execution Agents | Task execution | Produce concrete artifacts, evidence, and updates |
| Ops Transverse | Execution readiness | Gate operational launch and cross-domain handoffs |
| Donna/DLQ | Failure capture | Hold blocked, risky, or ownerless work |

## People Control Loop
1. Read the active B1 priority and B2 gate state.
2. Assign one accountable B2 owner per domain involved.
3. Assign one B3 execution agent per concrete JTBD.
4. Confirm capacity, decision rights, and proof format.
5. Route execution through Ops if the work touches delivery or launch.
6. Escalate to Donna/DLQ when ownership, proof, authority, or safety is missing.
7. Update the B3 roster, handoff log, and proof log after every material shift.

## Project Constraint
No field job may run without crew owner, supervisor, QA owner, backup, and client communication owner.

## People Metrics
- Domains with named B2 owners.
- Active B3 tasks with named agents.
- Ownerless blockers.
- Capacity overload signals.
- Handoff latency.
- Proof log freshness.

# JTBD-002 ALIKALY Transverse Legal Risk Gate  ## Job When 04 Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC prepares any B2 or B3 action across the eight domains, Legal must verify claims, data, IP, contract, authority, compliance, safety/liability, and approval boundaries before execution or launch.  ## Trigger Use this gate before: - publishing claims, campaigns, landing pages, offers, scripts, or public positioning; - handling client/member/childcare/entity/field/finance/legal data; - transferring deliverables, IP, documents, assets, or records; - signing, filing, billing, refunding, onboarding, or committing externally; - running work where authority, compliance, privacy, or liability is unclear.  ## Output A dated Legal readiness decision: - LEGAL_READY - NEEDS_REVIEW - BLOCKED_RISK  ## Readiness Checks | Check | Question | | --- | --- | | Claim | Is the promise supported and bounded? | | Data | What data is touched and how is it protected? | | IP | Who owns the deliverable, asset, content, or method? | | Terms | What agreement, waiver, refund, or commitment applies? | | Liability | What safety, compliance, or responsibility risk exists? | | Authority | Who can approve, sign, publish, or commit? | | External review | Is professional legal/tax/compliance review needed? | | Escalation | Where does uncertainty go? |  ## Guardrail No filing, entity, asset, family governance, finance, or public positioning action may proceed without legal authority, documentation, and J03 coordination path.  ## Proof Attach the Legal packet, decision log, boundary proof, cross-domain approvals, and final Legal decision.

# JTBD-002 ALIKALY Transverse Ops Gate

## Job
When Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC prepares an entity, filing, finance, or governance action, Ops must verify all eight B2 domains have given enough proof for B3 to execute in the right sequence.

## Output
A dated Ops readiness decision:
- `GO`
- `CONDITIONAL`
- `NO-GO`

## Readiness Checks
| Domain | Check |
| --- | --- |
| People | Authority and stakeholder owner |
| IT | Record and access control |
| Ops | Entity transition sequence |
| Product | Holding purpose and scope |
| Growth | External communication boundary |
| Finance | Tax/account/budget proof |
| Legal | Filing/agreement review path |
| Integration | J03 dependencies and blockers |

## Guardrail
No filing, asset transfer, tax, or governance action proceeds without Legal/Finance/J03 coordination.

## Proof
Attach the B2 handoff packet, blocker list, and final Ops decision.

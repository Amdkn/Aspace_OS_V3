# ALIKALY B2/B3 Finance Approval Protocol  ## Finance Packet Every B2/B3 task that touches price, cost, spend, payment, refund, tax, labor, tooling, margin, cash, quote, invoice, or financial commitment must carry this packet:  project: 04 Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC b2_domain: b2_owner: b3_agent: money_surface: price_or_budget: cost_components: margin_or_unit_economics: payment_terms: refund_or_exception_boundary: tax_or_legal_dependency: approval_owner: proof_required: decision:  ## Assignment Rules | Rule | Standard | | --- | --- | | One money surface | The financial category is explicit | | One approval owner | Spend/price/payment authority is named | | One model | Price, cost, and margin proof are attached | | One exception path | Refund/leakage/tax risk has escalation | | One decision | Ready, needs model, or blocked leakage |  ## Refusal Conditions Finance blocks execution when: - price or budget is missing; - cost-to-serve is unknown; - margin is negative or unproven; - payment/refund terms are vague; - labor or tooling is unpriced; - tax/legal/entity dependency is unresolved; - approval authority is unclear.  ## Output Finance emits one of three states: - FINANCE_READY: B3 can execute inside the named money boundary. - NEEDS_MODEL: work returns to Finance/B2 for pricing/cost proof. - BLOCKED_LEAKAGE: work routes to Donna/DLQ or B1.

# ALIKALY Ops Transverse Domain Operating System

## Purpose
Ops is the transverse gate for Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC. Its job is to sequence the eight B2 domains into a controlled entity-transformation operating system.

## Operating Thesis
Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC is a legal/entity transformation with cross-J01/J03 implications. Ops must sequence filings, banking, asset inventory, governance, and family coordination without inventing legal certainty.

## Transverse Flow
1. People confirms family/stakeholder roles and accountable decision owners.
2. IT confirms records, access, document storage, and identity controls.
3. Product confirms the operating purpose of the holding entity.
4. Growth confirms external positioning only after legal/finance boundaries.
5. Finance confirms taxes, accounts, budgets, and capital flows.
6. Legal confirms filings, entity state, agreements, and compliance path.
7. Ops converts inputs into sequence, checklist, decision log, and escalation.
8. B3 executes only after Ops issues the transverse readiness decision.

## Eight-Domain Ops Interface
| Domain | Ops Question | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| People | Who can decide and sign? | Role map and authority note |
| IT | Where are records controlled? | Storage/access map |
| Ops | What sequence prevents disorder? | Entity transition checklist |
| Product | What is the entity's operating purpose? | Purpose and scope note |
| Growth | What may be communicated externally? | Approved positioning boundary |
| Finance | What money/tax/accounting steps exist? | Finance checklist |
| Legal | What filings and agreements are required? | Legal review queue |
| Integration | What action needs J03 coordination? | Escalation and decision log |

## Project Constraint
Any tax, asset transfer, family governance, or filing action must route through Legal/Finance and J03 coordination before execution.

## Ops Metrics
- Filing readiness.
- Decision-owner clarity.
- Open legal/finance blockers.
- Record completeness.
- Cross-J03 coordination status.

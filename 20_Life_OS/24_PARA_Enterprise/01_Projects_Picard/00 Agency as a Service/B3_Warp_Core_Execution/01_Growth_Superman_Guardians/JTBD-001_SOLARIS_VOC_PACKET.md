# JTBD-001 - SOLARIS VOC Packet

## Job

When the **Solaris AaaS flagship** needs market traction, produce the VOC packet that proves who the audience is, what pain matters, what promise is safe, and which cross-domain boundaries must be respected before a Growth experiment runs.

> **VOC/ICP content (consolidated):** see companion fiche **`SOLARIS_ICP_MARKET_STUDY_2026.md`** (this folder)
> — recovered 2026 agency market study + formalized SDD-007 Solaris ICP + SOA/SOC/SLA backbone.
> ICP = **marketing/creative/web agencies** (NOT B2B SaaS).

## Output

1. Audience and ICP candidate.
2. VOC evidence and source notes.
3. Painkiller/value variant candidates.
4. Sales handoff requirement.
5. Product promise and exclusion boundary.
6. Ops delivery feasibility dependency.
7. IT tracking/source-of-truth requirement.
8. Finance budget or CAC constraint.
9. People owner/reviewer/capacity note.
10. Legal claim/privacy/compliance review note.

## Eight-Domain Growth Map

| Domain | Growth Surface | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | ICP, VOC, positioning, channels, experiments | Audience, promise, channel owner, metric, learning loop |
| Sales | Offer handoff, lead quality, follow-up | Qualified lead criteria and sales script boundary |
| Product | Promise-to-product fit | Feature/service promise matched to deliverable |
| Ops | Delivery feasibility | Ops launch gate before public promise scales |
| IT | Tracking, attribution, landing assets | Source of truth and measurement path |
| Finance | CAC, pricing signal, margin | Budget, unit economics, experiment spend cap |
| People | Growth owner, creator, reviewer | Capacity and approval ownership |
| Legal | Claims, privacy, compliance | Approved message boundary and risk review |

## Guardrails

- Do not write claims before VOC evidence.
- Do not define ICP so broadly that Sales cannot act.
- Do not promise what Product and Ops cannot deliver.
- Do not run untracked experiments.
- Do not spend without Finance boundary.
- Do not publish legal/compliance-sensitive claims without review.
- Project constraint: No campaign, offer promise, or acquisition push may launch without ICP, VOC, promise boundary, channel owner, metric, and Ops handoff.

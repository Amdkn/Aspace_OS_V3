## ICP Variant Determination Rules

### Mode = Orbiter (Field-First / Logistics) when:
- Primary offer is execution/delivery/operations for childcare
- Target client decides on reliability/track record/compliance assurance
- ASP > $50K, contract-heavy, relationship-driven
- Client profile: capacity-focused, long-term, franchise-oriented, regulatory-bound
- **Override for Child Care**: High liability + strong regulations = Orbiter primary regardless of ASP

### Mode = Nexus (Data-First / Expert Knowledge) when:
- Primary offer is compliance expertise/regulatory knowledge
- Target client decides on credentials/certification/compliance track record
- CAC < $500, ASP > $25K, sales cycle > 45 days
- Client profile: eval-intensive, evidence-driven, licensing-focused

### Mode = Solaris (Visual-First) when:
- Primary offer is parent-facing marketing/childcare brand
- Target client decides on aesthetics/parent experience
- CAC > $500, ASP varies, sales cycle < 30 days
- Client profile: parent-choice-driven, brand-conscious

**Override rule**: Jerry Prime can force mode shift with B1 approval when market signals diverge from revenue threshold.

**Childcare constraint**: Every offer must pass B2-G8 Legal compliance review before launch due to regulatory requirements.

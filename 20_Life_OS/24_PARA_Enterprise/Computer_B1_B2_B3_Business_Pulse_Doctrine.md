# Computer Doctrine - B1/B2/B3 Business Pulse In PARA

Computer keeps the Business Pulse fractal inside PARA so Jerry and Summer do not escape Life OS governance.

## PARA Placement

| Fractal | PARA owner | Location | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| Jerry | Spock | Areas | Ongoing responsibility, portfolio, standards, constraints |
| Summer | Picard | Projects | Finite execution, active project, 12WY cycle, Definition of Done |
| B2 DC managers | Inside Jerry or Summer | Domains | Own Rocks and tactic specifications |
| B3 Marvel squads | Inside Summer | Warp Core execution logs | Execute tactics only, produce Lead/Lag evidence |

## Chain Of Responsibility

```text
Jerry Area
  -> Cerritos clarifies raw idea
  -> Picard opens or updates Summer's Verse Project
  -> Summer B1 sets 1y/3y/10y vision and 4-cycle 12WY ownership
```

## Non-Negotiables

- B3 does not redefine Rocks.
- B3 does not own strategy.
- B2 does not hide execution without Lead/Lag logs.
- B1 does not bypass Beth, Batman, Finance, or Legal gates.
- B1 must exist as a direction cockpit before B2 can responsibly define domain Rocks.
- A Project folder without the 8 Business domains is structurally incomplete.
- Interface prototypes belong under Product until Ops/Batman and Fantastic Four define Build Gates and SOPs.
- Product can create a working artifact, but only the B2 gate matrix can promote it from `Product Done` to `Business Done`.
- If any non-Product B2 domain is empty for an active release, the project remains `PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE`.

## B2 Gate Matrix

Every Summer's Verse project must carry a `B2_DOMAIN_GATE_MATRIX.md` under `B2_Business_Domains/`.

Required gate order:

1. Product proves user value.
2. Ops proves repeatable delivery.
3. IT proves runtime, access, deployment, and backup boundaries.
4. Finance proves cost, price, and margin logic.
5. Legal proves claims, privacy, IP, and terms boundaries.
6. Sales proves qualification, objections, and handoff.
7. Growth proves ICP, message, channel, and measurement.
8. People proves ownership, training, handoff, and load.

This prevents Product from becoming the whole business by accident.

## B2 Swarm Supervision Rooms

Every B2 domain folder must carry three supervision artifacts. In Summer/Picard this lives under `B2_Business_Domains/`; in Jerry/Spock this lives under `B2_Area_Domains/`.

1. `00_B2_DOMAIN_CONTROL_ROOM.md` - domain mission, B2 responsibility, B2 anti-patterns, B3 swarm scope, and handoff rule.
2. `01_ROCK_TO_DOD_TO_JTBD_PIPELINE.md` - conversion path from B1 handoff to B2 Rock, DoD, acceptance criteria, Lead/Lag measures, and B3 jobs.
3. `02_B3_SWARM_SUPERVISION_PROTOCOL.md` - autonomy contract, JTBD packet, review loop, and Donna/DLQ safety exit.

B2 is a conductor, not a babysitter. It translates B1 vision into bounded goals, guardrails, and proof requirements. B3 chooses tactics, tools, sequencing, and exploration strategy as long as the JTBD and DoD remain intact.

The B2 review standard is evidence, not preference: artifacts, metrics, logs, links, screenshots, commits, or structured notes.

## B3 Squad Swarm Configurations

Every B3 execution surface must carry one folder per domain squad. In Summer/Picard this lives under `B3_Warp_Core_Execution/`; in Jerry/Spock this lives under `B3_Area_Warp_Core/`.

Each squad folder must contain:

1. `00_B3_SWARM_CONFIG.md` - squad topology, B2 boundary, internal graph, source inspiration, and operating rule.
2. `01_B3_AGENT_ROSTER.md` - Marvel/Illuminati member roles and peer-unlock rule.
3. `02_PEER_UNBLOCKING_AND_HANDOFFS.md` - internal handoff packet and escalation triggers.
4. `03_SHARED_CONTEXT_AND_PROOF_LOG.md` - shared context variables, artifact/proof paths, status, and productive disagreement rule.

B3 swarms collaborate as peers. A blocked B3 should first ask one member of the same squad to challenge the blocker and propose a workaround. B2 intervenes only when the DoD is ambiguous, the input does not exist, a cross-domain gate is touched, acceptance risk changes, or delegated authority is exceeded.

# Fable Wargame Kit

Companion resource for the video. Fable 5 leaves subscriptions on July 7 and moves to usage credits. This kit is how you bank its judgment before that happens: make it WARGAME your hardest projects on paper, then hand the battle plans to any cheaper model to execute.

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

1. Unzip and move the `fable-last-week` folder somewhere clean, for example `~/Documents/fable-last-week`.
2. Open `The Laundry List.pdf` and pick the mission that matches your week. 10 domains, each with a full prompt.
3. Open the matching file in `tasks/` and fill every `{{PLACEHOLDER}}` with your real details. The more specific the brief, the better the wargame.
4. Start Claude Code inside the folder (`cd ~/Documents/fable-last-week && claude`), then point it at the mission, for example "run tasks/01-website.md".
5. The battle plan lands in `wargames/`. Grade it against `SUCCESS.md` (all 8 points must hold) and log the run in `LEDGER.md`.

## What's inside

- `The Laundry List.pdf` - the visual guide: 10 wargame orders across 10 business domains with the full optimized prompts
- `fable-last-week/tasks/` - the 10 mission files, fill-in-the-blank briefs
- `fable-last-week/wargames/` - empty on purpose, your battle plans land here
- `fable-last-week/SUCCESS.md` - the 8-point standard every wargame must pass
- `fable-last-week/LEDGER.md` - one entry per mission, self-grades and patches

## The point

A wargame is a simulated course of action: every move with its expected observation, its likely failure and counter-move, every fork with a trigger, abort conditions, and the verification runs that prove it worked. Written so a mid-tier model can execute it blind, without asking a single question.

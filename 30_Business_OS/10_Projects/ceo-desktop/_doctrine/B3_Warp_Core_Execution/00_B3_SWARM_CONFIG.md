## Operating Rule (No-Babysitting)

- **B3 owns execution strategy.** B3 does the work.
- **B2 owns acceptance.** B2 reviews goals, DoD, gates, and proof.
- **B1 owns direction.** B1 (Summer / Jerry L2) sets north-star and arbitration.
- **Doctrine (P1–P18) is inherited from Area Jerry**, never re-derived here.
- B3 escalates to B2 **only** on: missing authority, missing inputs,
  cross-domain conflict, or DoD ambiguity. Every other step is owned locally.

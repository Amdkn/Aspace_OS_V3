# North Star — CEO's Desktop

> Reference: `../SUMMERS_VERSE_MANIFEST.md` for the full vision frame. This file is the B1 directional extract.

## 1-Year Direction

Lock the CEO's Desktop from skeleton to a working command center for A0 over the 8 SOB domains (Growth, Sales, Product, Ops, IT, Finance, People, Legal) and the 8 B3 squads that execute them.

Minimum outcomes:

- one A0 command surface — opens fast, shows where A0 is drifting;
- one B1 direction lock (this file) ratified;
- one B2 handoff queue seeded for the 8 SOB (see `04_B2_HANDOFF_QUEUE.md`);
- one 12WY cadence running two cycles (2026-Q3 → 2026-Q4);
- one decision charter with at least one logged decision;
- one junction to Life OS drafted (handoff spec, not yet wired).

## 3-Year Direction

CEO's Desktop becomes A0's **Life Wheel orchestrator**. The 12WY cadence is the heartbeat. B2 managers own their columns. B3 squads prove their work. A0 stops hero-moding and starts reading the dashboard.

Minimum outcomes:

- 8 SOB domains each with a named B2 manager and a current Rock;
- 8 B3 squads each operating a 12WY cycle with Lead/Lag indicators;
- a working junction to Life OS so A0 sees business drift and life drift in one scroll;
- delegated ownership: A0 no longer single-point-of-failure on any domain.

## 10-Year Direction

A0's **sovereign CEO runtime**. The desktop is licensable doctrine, not bespoke code. A future sovereign can install A'Space OS V2 and get the CEO's Desktop as part of the stack.

Minimum outcomes:

- licensable doctrine pack (B1/B2/B3 templates, handoff queue schema, decision charter);
- archive-ready project memory per the solaris graduation gates;
- portable across A'Space Multiverse (VPS, on-prem, air-gapped sovereign install);
- the business wheel rotation preserved across future agents, modes, and seasons.

## Direction Invariants

- B1 = A0. The CEO is the only B1.
- B2 = 8 SOB managers, one per domain. No shared B2.
- B3 = 8 squad leads, one Marvel/DC team per SOB.
- Beth & Morty safety can halt the desktop, but never override A0's direction.
- The desktop surfaces drift; it does not fix it. A0 fixes it.
- The 12WY cadence is non-negotiable. Drift in cadence = drift in business.
- No Product-only release can be marked Business Done without the 4 boundary gates (IT, Finance, Legal, People).

## Anti-Drift Reminders

- The desktop is not a product. Resist the urge to "ship features" to it.
- The desktop is not solaris. solaris is a portfolio project. The desktop is a command surface.
- The desktop is not a Notion clone. If the data lives only in Notion, it is not sovereign.

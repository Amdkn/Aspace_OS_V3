# 00 Agency as a Service Legal Rock - Transverse Risk Boundary OS

## Rock

Develop the Legal gate for 00 Agency as a Service as a transverse claims, data, IP, compliance, contract, liability, and authority operating system across all 8 B2 domains.

## Definition Of Done

- Claims boundary for Growth and Sales messages.
- Data/privacy/storage boundary for IT, Ops, People, and client/member/entity records.
- IP and deliverable ownership boundary for Product and external assets.
- Terms, waiver, refund, billing, tax, and liability handoff to Finance/Ops.
- Authority and role boundary for People and B3 agents.
- Compliance-sensitive escalation path and decision log.
- Donna/DLQ route for unsafe, ownerless, or legally uncertain work.
- Launch rule: no public claim, client/member/field/entity workflow, data path, contract, or deliverable transfer launches without Legal boundary proof.

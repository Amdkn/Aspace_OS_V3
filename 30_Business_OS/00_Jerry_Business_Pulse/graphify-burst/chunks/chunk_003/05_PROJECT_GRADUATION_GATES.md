# Project Graduation Gates

## Status Ladder

| Status | Meaning | Required Proof |
|---|---|---|
| RAW_IDEA | Concept exists. | Capture note or B1 queue. |
| MARKET_HYPOTHESIS | ICP and pain are stated. | Offer packet draft. |
| VALIDATION_SPRINT | Fast test underway. | Outreach, landing page, demo, or interview plan. |
| PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE | Product exists without full business wheel. | Product artifact + missing gates listed. |
| SOB_INCUBATION | B1/B2/B3 loop exists. | DoD, JTBD, SOP, domain gates. |
| OPERATING_BUSINESS_CANDIDATE | Repeatable business behavior emerges. | Lead source, delivery SOP, price/margin, owner map. |
| BUSINESS_DONE | Can operate as a self-operating business slice. | All 8 B2 gates accepted or explicitly deferred by B1. |

## Graduation Rule

Product alone can move a project to PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE. Only the B2 council can recommend BUSINESS_DONE, and B1 must accept the tradeoff against the North Star.

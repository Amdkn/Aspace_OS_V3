# JTBD-001 - RILCOT Price Margin Model

## Job

When 03 RILCOT Members Space OS has active work in any of the eight B2 domains, produce and maintain the price/margin/money boundary so every offer, workflow, spend, payment, refund, tax, or delivery action has financial proof before B3 execution or launch.

## Output

1. Price, quote, or budget boundary.
2. Cost components and cost owner.
3. Margin or unit economics model.
4. Payment terms and cash timing.
5. Refund, exception, or leakage boundary.
6. Tax/legal/entity dependency note where relevant.
7. Approval owner and decision log.
8. Donna/DLQ escalation for unsafe or ownerless financial risk.

## Eight-Domain Finance Map

| Domain | Finance Surface | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | CAC, channel spend, promotion economics | Spend cap, CAC proxy, learning metric |
| Sales | Price, quote, payment terms, discounts | Approved quote and payment boundary |
| Product | Unit economics, package margin, cost-to-serve | Price/margin model and exclusions |
| Ops | Labor, supplies, delivery cost, exceptions | Cost owner and leakage path |
| IT | Tooling, subscriptions, automation cost | System cost and approval owner |
| Finance | Budget, cash, accounts, tax/refund risk | Finance decision log and controls |
| People | Compensation, staffing load, capacity cost | Labor/capacity cost and approval path |
| Legal | Taxes, refunds, liabilities, entity obligations | Legal/Finance boundary and review route |

## Guardrails

- Do not launch unpriced work.
- Do not accept hidden labor or hidden tooling cost.
- Do not scale offers with unknown margin.
- Do not collect, refund, spend, or commit without terms and approval.
- Do not pretend tax/legal/entity certainty; escalate to Legal/J03 where needed.
- Project constraint: 
No member-space motion may launch without membership price/value, recurring cost, payment path, and retention economics.

## Proof

Attach the finance packet, model, decision log, proof notes, and escalation route here. Until then, Finance remains CONDITIONAL.

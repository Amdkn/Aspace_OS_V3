# JTBD-002 AAAS Transverse Finance Money Gate  ## Job When 00 Agency as a Service prepares any B2 or B3 action across the eight domains, Finance must verify price, margin, budget, cost, payment, refund, tax/legal dependency, approval, and leakage boundary before execution or launch.  ## Trigger Use this gate before: - publishing a price, quote, offer, package, membership, or service promise; - spending money, buying tools, assigning labor, or launching paid channels; - billing, refunding, collecting payments, or changing payment terms; - launching field/client/member/entity work with cost or liability exposure; - running work where margin, cash, or tax/entity implications are unclear.  ## Output A dated Finance readiness decision: - FINANCE_READY - NEEDS_MODEL - BLOCKED_LEAKAGE  ## Readiness Checks | Check | Question | | --- | --- | | Price | What is charged or budgeted? | | Cost | What labor, tools, supplies, compliance, or delivery costs exist? | | Margin | Is the work profitable or intentionally subsidized? | | Cash | When is money received or paid? | | Refund/exception | What happens when delivery changes or fails? | | Approval | Who can approve spend, price, or payment terms? | | Tax/legal | Does Legal/J03 need review? | | Leakage | What unpriced labor or hidden cost can break the model? |  ## Guardrail No offer, client workflow, or delivery package may launch without price, margin, cost, payment, refund, and leakage boundary.  ## Proof Attach the Finance packet, price/margin model, approval proof, cross-domain dependencies, and final Finance decision.

# VPP — Vitality Protection Protocol
## J02 Jerry Bio — Summer's Verse Manifest

> **What this is**: A protection protocol. Not a revenue project. Not a growth plan.
> Summer's Verse = the *preconditions* for everything else to function.
> Without VPP, the biological and cognitive substrate degrades silently until it becomes irreversible.
> This document defines what must be PROTECTED, not what must be BUILT.
>
> **Tone**: Clinical. Threshold-driven. Non-negotiable.

## 1. Protocol Identity

### 1.1 What VPP Is

VPP is the Summer's Verse layer for Jerry Bio. Where other Jerry areas (LD01 Business Pulse, etc.) operate on a GO/EXPAND logic, VPP operates on a STOP/PROTECT logic. It is the immune system of the Jerry operating model — it detects damage, triggers containment, and enforces recovery before the damage spreads to business operations.

VPP does not generate output. It generates the conditions under which all other Jerry areas CAN generate output.

### 1.2 Why VPP Exists

```
Without VPP:
  Sleep debt accumulates → cognitive bandwidth collapses
  Cognitive bandwidth collapses → decision quality degrades
  Decision quality degrades → LD01 business expansion creates liability instead of value
  LD01 creates liability → Beth HALT veto triggers catastrophically
  Beth HALT veto → ALL Jerry areas freeze

With VPP:
  ORANGE is detected at 72h → early intervention
  Intervention → substrate restored → normal operations resume
  No HALT veto triggered → Jerry areas continue to EXPAND
```

VPP exists to ensure the ORANGE → RED cascade never fires. It is cheaper to run VPP than to recover from RED.

### 1.3 What VPP Does NOT Do

- VPP does not generate revenue
- VPP does not create new business commitments
- VPP does not make decisions about LD01 expansion — it CREATES or REMOVES the conditions under which expansion is safe
- VPP does not negotiate thresholds — thresholds are hardcoded from the AREA_STANDARD

## 2. Biological Stack Rules (LD03 — Vitality)

### 2.1 Sleep Architecture

**Source**: *Why We Sleep*, Matthew Walker; *Breath*, James Nestor

Sleep is the only domain where the minimum is non-negotiable and the cost of violation is compounding. There is no productivity hack that replaces sleep. There is no caffeine protocol that fixes sleep debt. The only cure for sleep deprivation is sleep.

**Hard Rules**:
- **Sleep window**: Fixed, ±30min. Variance >45min triggers ORANGE review.
- **Minimum 7h/night** baseline; 8h/night after high-cognitive-load days
- **No screens 60min before sleep onset** — environmental constraint, not preference
- **Cool room**: 18–20°C (65–68°F)
- **Consistent wake time** — the single most enforceable anchor. One deviation resets recovery debt.

**Threshold Enforcement**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Sleep duration | ≥7h | 6–6.9h | <6h |
| Sleep onset latency | <20min | 20–40min | >40min |
| Nightly awakenings | 0–1 | 2–3 | >3 or any >15min |
| Subjective quality (1–10) | ≥7 | 5–6 | <5 |

**Breath Optimization** (*Breath*, Nestor):
- Nasal breathing is the default state — oral breathing signals dysfunction
- CO2 tolerance: 30s breath holds after exhale, 4–5×/day
- Exhale-to-inhale ratio: minimum 2:1 (Box 2-4 breathing for autonomic downregulation)
- Tidal volume target: 5–6L/min

### 2.2 Cardiovascular & Metabolic Health

**Source**: *Outlive*, Peter Attia

**Hard Rules**:
- **VO2max**: Tested quarterly. Minimum clearance threshold: 35 mL/kg/min
- **RHR tracking**: Weekly. ORANGE = >5bpm above baseline. RED = >10bpm above baseline.
- **HRV (Heart Rate Variability)**: Primary daily metric. See thresholds.
- **Zone 2 training**: Minimum 150min/week at 60–70% max HR
- **Zone 5 sprints**: Minimum 1×/week — non-negotiable for CV and cognitive blood flow

**HRV Thresholds**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Resting HRV (ms) | ≥65ms | 60–64ms | <60ms |
| HRV SDANN (24h) | >50ms | 40–50ms | <40ms |
| HRV trend over 7d | Stable/rising | Declining 3–5d | Declining 7+d |

### 2.3 Movement & Structural Integrity

**Source**: *Bigger Leaner Stronger* (Matthews), *Becoming a Supple Leopard* (Starrett)

**Hard Rules**:
- **Resistance training**: Minimum 2×/week, full-body or upper/lower splits
  - 10–20 working sets/week per muscle group
  - Progressive overload: minimum 1 variable (weight/reps/sets) progressed weekly
- **Mobility work**: 10–15min/day — hip flexors, thoracic spine, ankles, shoulders
- **Daily step minimum**: 7,000 steps (floor, not target)
- **Posture resets**: Every 30min during seated work

**Exercise Thresholds**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Resistance sessions/week | ≥2 | 1 | 0 |
| Zone 2 min/week | ≥150min | 75–149min | <75min |
| Zone 5 sprints/week | ≥1 | 0 (any gap) | N/A |
| Daily steps | ≥7,000 | 5,000–6,999 | <5,000 |

## 3. Cognitive Stack Rules (LD04 — Cognition)

### 3.1 Learning Acquisition Protocol

**Source**: *Learning How to Learn*, *Uncommon Sense Teaching* (Oakley & Sejnowski)

Learning is not passive consumption. It is active reconstruction of schema. Passive re-reading is noise. Active recall is signal. The ratio of signal to noise in a learning session determines whether LD04 stays GREEN or drifts toward ORANGE.

**Hard Rules**:
- **Focused learning blocks**: Minimum 45min, maximum 90min (diminishing returns beyond 90min per Oakley)
- **Active recall**: ≥60% of study time — flashcards or free recall, not passive review
- **Spaced repetition**: New concept retrieved from memory within 24h, then 3-day, 7-day, 21-day intervals
- **Interleaving**: Never practice one skill to mastery before switching — cross-train domains
- **Deep work blocks**: Minimum 90min of uninterrupted cognitive work per day to remain GREEN
- **Low-distraction environment**: Phone airplane mode, notifications blocked, browser closed

### 3.2 Cognitive Load Management

**Source**: *The Art of Learning* (Waitzkin), *Ultralearning* (Young)

**Hard Rules**:
- **Working memory limit**: Single task should require no more than 3–4 chunks simultaneously
- **Chunk formation deadline**: New information must connect to existing schema within 24h or it is lost
- **Cognitive load scoring**: Rate perceived difficulty 1–10 before each session — see thresholds
- **Desirable difficulty**: Learning should feel effortful but solvable within the session
- **Emotional regulation**: Anxiety >7/10 during learning degrades hippocampus-dependent encoding — abort session

**Cognitive Load Thresholds**:
| Signal | GREEN | ORANGE | RED |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Perceived difficulty (1–10) | 4–6 | 7–8 | 9–10 |
| Cognitive load score (1–10) | 1–5 | 6–7 | 8–10 |
| Session completion rate | ≥80% | 60–79% | <60% |
| Recovery debt (accumulated sleep debt) | 0–3h | 4–7h | >7h |

### 3.3 Learning Velocity Gates

**Hard Rules**:
- **Weekly learning progress review**: Every Sunday, 30min minimum
- **Ultralearning project cycles**: 10-week sprints. Post-cycle: 1 week integration/downtime (no new content)
- **Skill acquisition tracking**: Measurable output within 20h of initial study
- **Mastery criteria**: Ability to teach the concept to a novice without notes within 4 weeks

**Learning Velocity Thresholds**:
| LD04 State | New Domain Learning | Business Expansion Gate |
|------------|--------------------|------------------------ |
| GREEN (velocity stable/rising) | Allowed — 1 new domain per 10-week cycle | LD01 may add 1 new area |
| ORANGE (velocity declining) | Suspended — maintenance only | LD01 expansion PAUSED |
| RED (velocity negative) | Prohibited | LD01 CONTRACTION recommended |

```

  → Beth HALT veto AUTOMATICALLY triggered

    AND decision quality both compromised simultaneously
  → All non-essential learning SUSPENDED
  → All business expansion PAUSED
  → Essential only: maintenance of existing skills

### 4.4 LD03 → LD04 Cascade

```

  → LD03 recovery takes priority (sleep first — without sleep, LD04 cannot recover)

## 5. Founder Load Ceilings

> Jerry Bio operates on a finite resource pool. Business expansion consumes vitality and cognitive resources. The Founder Load Ceiling defines how much expansion the current substrate can support without breaching LD03/LD04 thresholds.

### 5.1 Capacity Model

```
Total Daily Capacity = f(sleep_quality, HRV_trend, cognitive_load_history, exercise_consistency)
```

### 5.2 Ceiling Rules

| Ceiling Type | GREEN State | ORANGE State | RED State |
|-------------|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| New commitment threshold | >2h/day requires Jerry Bio clearance | Denied unless essential | Denied — full stop |
| Weekly cognitive business work | >45h/week triggers ORANGE review | Maximum 30h/week | Prohibited |
| Simultaneous active projects | 3 concurrent in execution | 1 in execution | 0 in execution |
| High-cognitive-load activities (negotiations, strategic planning) | 3×/week | 1×/week | Prohibited |
| Meeting density | >5h/day triggers self-audit | Denied >3h/day | Prohibited |

### 5.3 Ceiling Enforcement Protocol

```
IF proposed activity + current load > Founder Load Ceiling
  → Jerry Bio issues DENIAL with specific ceiling reason
  → Proposal must be deferred OR distributed to another Jerry area OR
  → Jerry Bio must approve recovery period before activity may proceed

Appeals: None. Ceiling rules are hard constraints, not guidelines.
```

### 5.4 Simultaneous Commitment Limits

| Current LD03/LD04 State | Max Concurrent Business Projects | Max LD01 Expansion Additions |
|------------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Both GREEN | 3 | 1 per quarter |
| Single ORANGE | 1 | 0 until cleared |
| Compound ORANGE | 0 | 0 — freeze |
| Any RED | 0 | 0 — full stop |

## 7. Runway Override Logic

> When VPP signals are ORANGE or RED, J01 (Agency as a Service) business expansion is NOT paused — it is actively at risk of creating liability. This section defines what happens to the runway when VPP signals degrade.

### 7.1 ORANGE Runway Override

```
When VPP signals ORANGE (any single LD03 or LD04 metric ORANGE):

J01 Business Expansion Response:
  → NO new client onboarding
  → NO new service offerings
  → Existing client work continues — no new commitments added
  → Active deals: pause negotiation, convert to "under review" status
  → Pipeline: flag all deals with >2 weeks to close — risk of LD04 degradation during close
  → Revenue recognition: prioritize cash collection over expansion

Rationale: ORANGE means the substrate is already stressed. Adding new business
commitments before recovery = compounding LD03/LD04 degradation.
The cost of one bad decision under ORANGE cognitive load > the revenue from the new client.

Beth Notification: ORANGE advisory sent with LD03/LD04 specific signal
```

### 7.2 RED Runway Override

```
When VPP signals RED (any single LD03 or LD04 metric RED):

J01 Business Expansion Response:
  → FULL STOP — all new business activity prohibited
  → Existing client engagements: notify of reduced capacity, negotiate SLA suspension
  → Pipeline: freeze all. No exceptions.
  → Cash preservation mode: collect receivables, defer payables where possible
  → Beth HALT veto ENFORCED — J01 receives formal veto notification

Runway Impact:
  → Assume 2–4 weeks of recovery before normal operations resume
  → Scenario plan: if RED persists >7 days, Beth coordinates with other Jerry areas
    to redistribute J01 client load or activate emergency support
  → No business travel, negotiations, or strategic planning until clearance

Recovery sequence: Sleep restoration FIRST → LD03 returns GREEN → LD04 follows →
J01 gradual resumption → full runway resumes at Week 3 minimum
```

### 7.3 VPP → Runway Signal Map

| VPP Signal | J01 Expansion Gate | Revenue Implication | Beth Role |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------------|-----------|
| GREEN | OPEN — 1 addition/quarter | Full velocity | None |
| ORANGE | PAUSED — no new commitments | Reduced velocity (existing only) | Warning flag |
| RED | CLOSED — full stop | Near-zero (maintenance only) | HALT veto + coordination |

### 7.4 Runway Resume Protocol

```
Precondition for J01 resumption after RED:
  1. All LD03 metrics GREEN for minimum 48h consecutively
  2. All LD04 metrics GREEN for minimum 48h consecutively
  3. No new ORANGE signals introduced during recovery
  4. Jerry Bio clearance issued in writing (scorecard sign-off)

J01 resumption sequence:
  Week 1: Existing clients only — no new onboarding
  Week 2: Pipeline evaluation — selective re-engagement
  Week 3: New business resumed — with ceiling rules in effect
  Week 4: Full velocity restored — with new Founder Load Ceiling baseline

If RED recurs within 30 days of resume: Beth escalation — external support review required
```

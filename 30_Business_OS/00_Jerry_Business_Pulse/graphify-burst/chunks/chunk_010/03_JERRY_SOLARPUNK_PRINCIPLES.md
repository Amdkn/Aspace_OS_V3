## Cluster A — Relational Capital (LD05/01) — *Carnegie* · *Give and Take* · *Speed of Trust*
**SP1 — Give first (Grant's Givers win long-term).** Generosity is the activation energy of relational capital; be the first to give. (AREA_STANDARD LD05 Rule 5.)
**SP2 — Be genuinely interested; remember names & details (Carnegie).** Track relational assets with the rigor others track financial ones.
**SP3 — Trust is the hidden multiplier (Covey).** High trust = speed + low cost; every interaction either deposits or withdraws from the trust account.

## Cluster B — Network Activation (LD05/02) — *Voss* · *5 Love Languages*
**SP4 — Activate the network toward solarpunk objectives, not only commercial ones.** The network is deployed for impact, not just deals (distinguishes J04 from Sales/Growth).
**SP5 — Speak each person's language.** Tailor the ask/recognition to how they actually receive value (Voss tactical empathy + Chapman) — relational, not transactional.

## Cluster C — Experiential Capital / Flow (LD07/03) — *Flow* (Csikszentmihalyi) · *Play* · *Power of Fun*
**SP6 — Design for flow.** Match challenge to skill; flow is where authentic experience + creative output live (mirror Bio BIO25 soft-zone).
**SP7 — Fun is fuel, not frivolity (McGonigal/Brown).** Play and authentic fun regenerate the operator and the community — protected, not earned-after-work.

## Cluster D — Creativity Gates (LD07/04) — *Art of Game Design* + the discipline mindset
**SP8 — Ship the creation; beat the resistance.** Creativity is a discipline of finishing and releasing (echo People PE21 iteration / Abou: the freestyle drilled then *performed*).
**SP9 — The Creativity Gate: does it create authentic experience?** An artifact passes only if it produces real flow/fun/meaning — not mere engagement bait. (AREA_STANDARD boundary.)

## Cluster E — Solarpunk / Regenerative Doctrine (LD08/05) — *Benyus* · *Lerner* · *MacArthur* · *Pauli* · *Aberkane*
**SP10 — Biomimicry: nature is the prior art and the teacher (Benyus).** Design regenerative solutions modeled on living systems (echo Legal L15 biomimetic IP).
**SP11 — Urban acupuncture: small precise interventions heal the whole (Lerner).** High-leverage micro-actions over grand plans (echo Legal L17).
**SP12 — Circular + Blue economy: design out waste, cascade resources (MacArthur/Pauli).** Cradle-to-cradle loops; one output is another's input (echo Legal L16).
**SP13 — Knowledge is the inexhaustible resource (Aberkane) + Low-Tech sobriety.** Abundance through knowledge + Smart High-Low Tech, not consumption. (Echoes the Finance Kardashev ceiling + Legal abundance L13-L18 — same civilizational thesis, the Life-side mirror.)

## Cluster F — MUSE Eligibility (LD08/06)
**SP14 — Only public-benefit work earns MUSE promotion.** The V0→V1 "Muse" graduation (Symphony SDD-010) requires a real contribution artifact, not revenue alone (AREA_STANDARD MUSE criteria).
**SP15 — Measure impact artifacts, not just revenue.** Track what was contributed to the commons (the public-benefit ledger), the way Finance tracks margin.

## Cluster G — Public Benefit Standards (LD08/07)
**SP16 — Revenue is the floor, contribution is the qualifier.** Every L2 project answers "what does this contribute to the solarpunk commons?" — "nothing beyond revenue" disqualifies it as Solarpunk output. (AREA_STANDARD boundary rule.)
**SP17 — Build for the 10,000-year horizon.** Frameworks meant to outlast (echo Legal L18 Federation frame) — civilization, not just a quarter.

## Cluster H — Contribution Architecture / Transmission (LD08/08 + the video)
**SP18 — Pass the flame (the cypher law).** "Rentre dans le Cercle": you take the mic because someone gave it to you → you transmit (mentor, dédicace, relais). Transmission is the output, not a byproduct. (A0 value-canon + child-care/diaspora reality.)
**SP19 — Be ready to receive the good as much as the hard.** "Les belles choses et les baracas, des fois c'est plus dur à accueillir que les galères" — readiness to accept success/opportunity is a discipline (ties Bio discipline + foi).
**SP20 — Community over audience.** Build a cercle (mutual, named, present), not a follower count — relational + impact capital compound where audience metrics don't.

## Cluster I — Cross-domain authority
**SP21 — Solarpunk vetoes pure-extraction.** A project generating engagement/revenue with zero commons contribution does not qualify; Jerry Solarpunk widens the gate (like Legal's abundance veto) — revenue is necessary, never sufficient.
**SP22 — Foi + mission are the north star.** The whole stack serves the mission (leave a mark, serve the diaspora/commons) under faith — not the inverse. (A0 value-canon.)

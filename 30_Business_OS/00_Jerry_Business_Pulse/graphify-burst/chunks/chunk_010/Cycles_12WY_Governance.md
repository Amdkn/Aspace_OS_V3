# B1 Summer Direction - 4 Cycles 12WY Governance

Each Summer's Verse is governed as a one-year arc made of four 12WY cycles.

## Vision Fields

```yaml
vision_1y: ""
vision_3y: ""

```

## Four Cycles

| Cycle | Summer responsibility | Output |
|---|---|---|
| C1 - Foundation | Establish Product/Ops baseline and first proof | Build gate, first MUSE candidate, initial scorecard |
| C2 - Stabilization | Reduce manual dependency and stabilize SOPs | Ops SOP, Finance guard, Legal baseline |
| C3 - Growth | Scale only after Batman/Ops and Beth permit | Growth/Sales engine, qualified pipeline |
| C4 - MUSE Graduation | Convert project into reusable asset or archive | DEAL decision, Data archive packet, Jerry update |

## Gate Rule

If Beth is HALT, C2/C3/C4 expansion stops. B3 may close in-flight tactics but may not accept new work.

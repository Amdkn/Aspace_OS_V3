# ALIKALY Growth Transverse Demand Signal OS

## Purpose
Growth is the transverse demand gate for 04 Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC. It converts market signal into qualified audiences, promises, channels, experiments, and learning loops without breaking Sales, Product, Ops, IT, Finance, People, or Legal constraints.

## Operating Thesis
Alikaly needs Growth to define external positioning carefully while protecting legal, family governance, finance, and entity-transition boundaries.

## Eight-Domain Growth Interface
| Domain | Growth Surface | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | ICP, VOC, positioning, channels, experiments | Audience, promise, channel owner, metric, learning loop |
| Sales | Offer handoff, lead quality, follow-up | Qualified lead criteria and sales script boundary |
| Product | Promise-to-product fit | Feature/service promise matched to deliverable |
| Ops | Delivery feasibility | Ops launch gate before public promise scales |
| IT | Tracking, attribution, landing assets | Source of truth and measurement path |
| Finance | CAC, pricing signal, margin | Budget, unit economics, experiment spend cap |
| People | Growth owner, creator, reviewer | Capacity and approval ownership |
| Legal | Claims, privacy, compliance | Approved message boundary and risk review |

## Growth Control Loop
1. Capture VOC and market signal.
2. Filter through ICP and project-specific constraints.
3. Create painkiller/value variants.
4. Select channels and experiment format.
5. Confirm Sales handoff and Product promise boundary.
6. Confirm Ops delivery feasibility and IT measurement path.
7. Confirm Finance budget/unit economics and Legal message boundary.
8. Assign People owner/reviewer and launch only after the gate decision.

## B2/B3 Integration
| Layer | Growth Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| B1 Summer Direction | Keep market motion aligned to project priority |
| B2 Domain Owners | Provide constraints before promise goes public |
| B3 Execution Agents | Produce VOC, ICP, variants, experiments, and proof |
| Sales | Receives qualified signal and offer boundaries |
| Product/Ops | Confirms promise can be built and delivered |
| IT/Finance/Legal/People | Confirms tracking, economics, claims, and ownership |
| Donna/DLQ | Catches unsupported, unsafe, or ownerless growth motion |

## Readiness States
- `GROWTH_READY`: campaign or experiment can run within the named boundaries.
- `NEEDS_SIGNAL`: return to VOC/ICP because evidence is weak.
- `BLOCKED_PROMISE`: route to Ops/Product/Legal/Finance because the promise is unsafe or unsupported.

## Project Constraint
No external growth or positioning action may launch without Legal/Finance boundary, authority owner, and approved public narrative.

## Growth Metrics
- VOC evidence count and freshness.
- ICP clarity.
- Qualified lead or member signal.
- Experiment cycle time.
- Conversion by channel.
- CAC or acquisition cost proxy.
- Promise mismatch incidents.

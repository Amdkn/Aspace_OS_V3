# Project Graduation Gates - 04_Alikaly

## Purpose

This gate keeps the project from confusing activity, product, and business maturity.

## Gate Ladder

| Gate | Required Evidence | Decision Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Gate 0 - Direction | North Star, 12WY intent, B2 handoff exists. | B1 |
| Gate 1 - Market | ICP, pain, offer, validation sprint evidence. | Growth + Sales + B1 |
| Gate 2 - Product | Working artifact, user value, QA evidence. | Product |
| Gate 3 - Delivery | SOP, support path, ops runbook. | Ops |
| Gate 4 - Runtime | repo/deploy/access/backup boundaries. | IT |
| Gate 5 - Margin | price, cost, margin, payment path. | Finance |
| Gate 6 - Trust | claims, privacy, IP, terms boundaries. | Legal |
| Gate 7 - Handoff | owner map, training, no founder-only blocker. | People |

## Promotion Rule

A project can be promoted only to the highest gate with evidence. Missing evidence must be named as a blocker, not hidden as progress.

## Business Done

Business Done requires all 8 B2 domains to accept the release or to document a conscious B1 exception with a date for revisit.

# 12WY Command Cycles — CEO's Desktop

> **Phase 1 covers 2026-Q3 to 2026-Q4 with 2 cycles of 12WY.** Cycle boundaries to be ratified in Phase 2 once B2 managers are named.

## Why 12WY

The 12-Week Year is the heartbeat of the CEO's Desktop. A0 does not run on a calendar quarter (too long) or a week (too granular). The 12WY cycle is the cadence at which the 8 SOB domains report up and the desktop shows drift.

## Phase 1 — Direction Lock Cycles (2026-Q3 → 2026-Q4)

| Cycle | Window | Goal | Gate |
|-------|--------|------|------|
| **C1 — Direction Lock** | 2026-Q3 W1–W12 | Convert the Phase 1 skeleton into a ratified B1 cockpit | North Star ratified; 12WY cycles defined; decision charter live; handoff queue seeded for all 8 SOB |
| **C2 — Domain Activation Prep** | 2026-Q4 W1–W12 | Make the 8 SOB owners real enough to accept B1 handoffs | B2 manager named per SOB (or PENDING + risk note); first 1–2 B2 entries moved from PENDING → ACCEPTED with DoD draft |

## Phase 2+ — Cadence Cycles (Stub)

The full 4-cycle pattern (C1 Direction Lock → C2 Domain Activation → C3 Execution Proof → C4 Graduation Or Archive) follows the solaris reference. Full spec deferred to Phase 2 — this file is a stub for the cadence rhythm only.

## Weekly Review Prompt

> What did B1 (A0) decide, what did B2 accept, what did B3 prove, and what remains unsafe to scale across the 8 SOB?

## Anti-Drift Reminders

- A 12WY cycle that has no weekly review is dead. Book the slot.
- A 12WY cycle that produces no decision-charter entry is theatre. Log or skip.
- A 12WY cycle that has all 8 SOB in PENDING is a smell. Either activate or archive.

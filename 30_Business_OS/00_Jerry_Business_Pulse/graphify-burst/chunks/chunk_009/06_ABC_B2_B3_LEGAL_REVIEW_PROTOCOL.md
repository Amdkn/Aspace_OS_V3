# ABC B2/B3 Legal Review Protocol  ## Legal Packet Every B2/B3 task that touches a claim, contract, client/member/entity/childcare/field data, IP, billing/refund/tax responsibility, public message, or authority boundary must carry this packet:  project: 02 ABC OS & Child Care BOS b2_domain: b2_owner: b3_agent: legal_surface: claim_or_commitment: data_or_record_path: ip_or_deliverable_boundary: terms_or_contract_boundary: liability_or_safety_boundary: authority_owner: review_needed: proof_required: decision:  ## Assignment Rules | Rule | Standard | | --- | --- | | One legal surface | The risk category is explicit | | One authority owner | Approval and responsibility are named | | One proof path | Decision evidence is attached | | One escalation | Uncertain work routes to Legal/B1/Donna | | One boundary | Allowed, conditional, blocked, or external review |  ## Refusal Conditions Legal blocks execution when: - public claims are unsupported; - data path or privacy boundary is unclear; - IP or deliverable ownership is vague; - terms, waiver, refund, tax, or liability boundary is missing; - authority to decide/sign/commit is unclear; - external professional review is needed but absent.  ## Output Legal emits one of three states: - LEGAL_READY: B3 can execute inside the named boundary. - NEEDS_REVIEW: work returns to Legal/B2 for clarification. - BLOCKED_RISK: work routes to Donna/DLQ or B1.

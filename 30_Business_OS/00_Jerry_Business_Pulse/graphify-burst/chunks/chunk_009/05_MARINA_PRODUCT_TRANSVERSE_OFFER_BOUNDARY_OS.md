# MARINA Product Transverse Offer Boundary OS  ## Purpose Product is the transverse offer boundary gate for 05 Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP. It converts demand and sales intent into a scoped, testable, deliverable, supportable, priced, and legally bounded product/service system.  ## Operating Thesis Marina needs Product to package cleaning services, SOPs, QA, equipment, exclusions, and client walkthrough criteria.  ## Eight-Domain Product Interface | Domain | Product Surface | Required Proof | | --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | Market signal and value promise | VOC/ICP and promise evidence |
| Sales | Offer packaging and proposal fit | Scope/exclusions and closeable package |
| Product | Deliverable, MVP/demo, QA | Acceptance criteria and proof boundary |
| Ops | Delivery workflow and support | SOP, handoff, incident path |
| IT | Runtime, tooling, data | System/access/source-of-truth boundary |
| Finance | Unit economics and pricing | Cost, margin, price link |
| People | Builder/reviewer/operator roles | Owner, reviewer, capacity proof |
| Legal | IP, claims, terms, compliance | Ownership and legal boundary |

## Product Control Loop
1. Read Growth signal and Sales diagnostic.
2. Define product/service/package scope and exclusions.
3. Define MVP, demo, proof, or acceptance boundary.
4. Confirm Ops delivery, IT runtime, Finance price/margin, People ownership, and Legal/IP/claims boundary.
5. Emit Product readiness before Sales proposal, Ops launch, or B3 build.

## Readiness States
- PRODUCT_READY: offer/package/demo can proceed inside named boundary.
- NEEDS_SCOPE: return to Product/B2 because scope or acceptance is weak.
- BLOCKED_DELIVERY: route to Ops/IT/Finance/Legal/B1 because delivery is unsafe.

## Project Constraint
No field service package launches without SOP, QA checklist, equipment/safety boundary, pricing link, and Ops handoff.

## Product Metrics
- Scope clarity.
- Acceptance criteria completeness.
- Demo/proof readiness.
- QA gaps.
- Delivery mismatch incidents.
- Price-to-scope alignment.

# JTBD-001 - MARINA Claims Data IP Boundary

## Job

When 05 Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP has active work in any of the eight B2 domains, produce and maintain the claims/data/IP/legal boundary so every public claim, data workflow, deliverable, contract, authority, and liability surface has proof before B3 execution or launch.

## Output

1. Claims and public message boundary.
2. Data/privacy/storage/access boundary.
3. IP and deliverable ownership boundary.
4. Terms, waiver, contract, refund, tax, and liability notes where relevant.
5. Authority owner and approval path.
6. External review need, if any.
7. Legal decision log and proof path.
8. Donna/DLQ escalation for unsafe or ownerless risk.

## Eight-Domain Legal Map

| Domain | Legal Surface | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | Public claims, campaigns, privacy, endorsements | Approved claim boundary and privacy note |
| Sales | Offers, quotes, intake, terms, client commitments | Terms boundary and accepted responsibility |
| Product | IP, deliverables, exclusions, warranties | Ownership and scope/exclusion proof |
| Ops | SOPs, incidents, liability, safety, records | Liability/safety/escalation boundary |
| IT | Data handling, access, records, retention | Privacy/storage/access boundary |
| Finance | Billing, refunds, taxes, entity obligations | Payment/refund/tax responsibility boundary |
| People | Roles, contractors, consent, training, authority | Role authority and worker/agent boundary |
| Legal | Compliance, contracts, IP, defense, approvals | Legal review queue and decision log |

## Guardrails

- Do not publish unsupported claims.
- Do not move sensitive data without boundary proof.
- Do not transfer deliverables without IP/ownership clarity.
- Do not accept terms, refunds, taxes, safety, or liability ambiguity.
- Do not let B3 agents imply legal certainty.
- Project constraint: 
No field job or client promise may launch without service terms, safety/liability boundary, insurance/permission check, and escalation path.

## Proof

Attach the legal packet, decision log, proof notes, and escalation route here. Until then, Legal remains CONDITIONAL.

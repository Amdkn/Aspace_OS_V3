# Who Not How Delegation Matrix

## Purpose

This matrix prevents A0 from becoming the default executor whenever a project becomes unclear.

## Delegation Slots

| Question | Assigned Layer | Output |
|---|---|---|
| What is the strategic outcome? | B1 | North Star + 12WY intent. |
| Who owns the domain result? | B2 | Rock, DoD, acceptance proof. |
| Who executes the task? | B3 | JTBD output + Lead/Lag proof. |
| Which tool reduces manual work? | B2 IT + relevant B3 | Tool boundary and runbook. |
| What blocks delegation? | Donna/DLQ + B2 | Blocker class and next gate. |

## Delegation Rule

Before A0 personally solves a recurring problem, Jerry must ask which B2 owner, B3 squad, CLI, MCP, SOP, or automation should own the next repeatable version.

## Escalation

Escalate to B1 only when the desired outcome, risk appetite, authority boundary, or North Star changed. Do not escalate because an implementation step is merely uncomfortable.

# 00 Agency as a Service Sales Rock - Transverse Revenue Handoff OS

## Rock

Develop the Sales gate for 00 Agency as a Service as a transverse diagnostic, qualification, proposal, close, handoff, and revenue operating system across all 8 B2 domains.

## Definition Of Done

- Diagnostic and qualification criteria.
- Proposal scope, exclusions, price, and decision path.
- Growth lead-source and promise boundary.
- Product offer fit and deliverable boundary.
- Ops delivery feasibility handoff.
- Finance quote/payment/margin boundary.
- Legal claims/terms/commitment review.
- People owner/reviewer/support handoff.
- Launch rule: no external proposal or commitment leaves Sales until Product, Ops, Finance, and Legal gates confirm their boundaries.

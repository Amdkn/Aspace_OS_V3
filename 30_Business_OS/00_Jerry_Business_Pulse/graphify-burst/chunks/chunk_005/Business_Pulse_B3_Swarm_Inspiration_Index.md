- `https://github.com/openai/swarm`: educational inspiration for agent handoffs and composable routines.
- `https://github.com/VRSEN/agency-swarm`: inspiration for explicit agency roles and communication flows.

- `https://www.kimi.com/blog/agent-swarm`: inspiration for parallel agent work and productive disagreement.

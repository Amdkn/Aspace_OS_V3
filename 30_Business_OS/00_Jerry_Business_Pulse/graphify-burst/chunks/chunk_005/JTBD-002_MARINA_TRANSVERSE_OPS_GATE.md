# JTBD-002 MARINA Transverse Ops Gate

## Job
When Marina Cleaning BOS & SOP prepares a field job, Ops must verify all eight B2 domains have given enough proof for B3 to execute safely and profitably.

## Output
A dated Ops readiness decision:
- `GO`
- `CONDITIONAL`
- `NO-GO`

## Readiness Checks
| Domain | Check |
| --- | --- |
| People | Crew, training, supervisor |
| IT | Schedule, route, record/photo path |
| Ops | Field SOP and QA checklist |
| Product | Package, scope, exclusions |
| Growth | Client promise and intake |
| Finance | Quote, labor/supply cost, payment |
| Legal | Insurance, safety, terms |
| Integration | Blockers and job risks |

## Guardrail
No field job proceeds without crew capacity, safety proof, equipment readiness, and QA path.

## Proof
Attach the B2 handoff packet, blocker list, and final Ops decision.

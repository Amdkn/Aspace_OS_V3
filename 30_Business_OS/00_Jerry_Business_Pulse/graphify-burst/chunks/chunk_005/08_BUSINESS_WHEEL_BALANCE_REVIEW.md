# Business Wheel Balance Review

This review lets B1 keep the 8-domain Business Wheel balanced without becoming the operating bottleneck.

## Weekly Scan

| Domain | B2 | Status | Signal | B1 question |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Growth | Superman | green/yellow/red | ICP/message/channel proof | Is attention aligned with the offer? |
| Sales | Martian Manhunter / John Jones | green/yellow/red | qualification/conversion proof | Can trust convert without heroic effort? |
| Product | Flash | green/yellow/red | artifact/user-value proof | Is value inspectable and releasable? |
| Ops | Batman | green/yellow/red | SOP/repeatability proof | Can delivery repeat without chaos? |
| IT | Cyborg | green/yellow/red | runtime/access/backup proof | Can the system survive real use? |
| Finance | Wonder Woman | green/yellow/red | cost/margin/billing proof | Does the model protect margin? |
| People | Green Lantern | green/yellow/red | ownership/load/training proof | Is the work sustainably owned? |
| Legal | Aquaman | green/yellow/red | claim/privacy/IP proof | Is exposure bounded? |

## Balance Rules

- Product green with Ops/IT/Finance/Legal red means PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE.
- Growth green with Sales red means attention is not yet business traction.
- Sales green with Ops red means promises may outrun delivery.
- Product green with People red means founder-load risk.
- Finance red overrides growth speed until burn and margin are visible.
- Legal red blocks public claims, launch, and outbound promises.

## Output

B1 produces only three possible outputs:

1. NO_CHANGE: B2s continue.
2. B2_MANDATE: one or more domains receive a new mandate packet.
3. STRATEGIC_REBALANCE: North Star, cycle priority, or risk appetite changes.

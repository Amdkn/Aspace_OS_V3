## Cluster A — Capital Architecture (LD02/01) — *Psychology of Money* · *Rich Dad* · *Millionaire Fastlane*
**NX1 — Asset vs Liability, every decision.** Asset puts money in monthly; liability takes it out. Revenue that builds dependency without equity = liability; reject value-extraction that builds nothing. (AREA_STANDARD Rule J02-001.)
**NX2 — Climb the wealth tiers, never fall below without sign-off.** T1 Safety (6mo cash) → T2 Independence → T3 Acceleration → T4 Sovereignty (passive > 3× burn). No expansion drops your tier without explicit A0 sign-off. (AREA_STANDARD §2.)
**NX3 — Build equity in the Fastlane, not the Slowlane (DeMarco).** Prefer compounding business equity / owned assets over time-for-money; every Summer proposal declares its lane. (Echo Finance F14/F20 ownership.)

## Cluster B — Cash Flow Architecture (LD02/02) — *I Will Teach You to Be Rich* · *Simple Path* · *Your Money or Your Life*
**NX4 — Automate the money (Sethi).** Pay-yourself-first, automated splits (safety / invest / give), so discipline doesn't depend on willpower (mirror People PE19 systems-not-willpower).
**NX5 — Coverage ratio is the daily survival truth.** revenue ÷ essential burn; runway in months is the metric that gates everything (mirror Finance F1). ORANGE/RED auto-overrides Summer expansion. (AREA_STANDARD closure rule.)
**NX6 — Know your "Enough" (Your Money or Your Life).** Money = life energy; define the number that buys freedom, beyond which more is vanity. Foi over greed.

## Cluster C — Risk Management (LD02/03)
**NX7 — Survive first; never risk what you can't afford to lose.** Asymmetric downside protection before upside chasing (Housel: avoiding ruin compounds).

## Cluster F — Succession Planning (LD06/06) — *héritage* (A0 diaspora + Abou family)

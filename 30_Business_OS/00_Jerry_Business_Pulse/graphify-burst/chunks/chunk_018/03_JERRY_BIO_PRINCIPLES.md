## Cluster A — Sleep & Recovery (LD03/01) — *Why We Sleep* (Walker)
**BIO1 — Sleep is the foundation, not the leftover.** It's upstream of HRV, cognition, mood, immunity. Protect it first; everything else is downstream. (Thresholds: AREA_STANDARD §2.1.)
**BIO2 — The fixed wake-time is the single anchor.** One deviation resets recovery debt. The wake-time is non-negotiable even after a bad night — it stabilises the circadian system.
**BIO3 — Recovery debt is real and compounds.** Missed sleep accumulates (AREA_STANDARD tracks it); a RED sleep signal triggers the Beth HALT, not a "push through." Rest is a discipline, not a weakness (Abou: recovery is part of training).

## Cluster B — Breath (LD03/02) — *Breath* (Nestor)
**BIO4 — Nasal breathing is the default state.** Mouth-breathing is dysfunction, not preference — it degrades sleep, CO2 tolerance, and autonomic balance.
**BIO5 — Train CO2 tolerance as a resilience marker.** Breath-hold capacity (≥30s) is a daily KR; it's the cheapest measurable resilience signal.
**BIO6 — Exhale to downregulate.** A 2:1 exhale:inhale ratio is the on-demand off-switch for the stress response — the operator's tool before a high-stakes call/negotiation (ties Founder Load).

## Cluster C — Cardio & Longevity (LD03/03) — *Outlive* (Attia)
**BIO7 — Train for the Centenarian Decathlon.** Define the physical tasks you want at 90, then reverse-engineer the training now. VO2max is the strongest longevity lever — protect it (quarterly test, floor 35).
**BIO8 — Zone 2 is the metabolic floor, Zone 5 the ceiling.** ≥150min/week Zone 2 + ≥1 Zone 5 sprint/week — non-negotiable for cardiovascular AND cognitive blood flow.
**BIO9 — HRV is the daily autonomic truth-teller.** It's the primary daily metric; a declining 7-day trend is an early STOP signal before you "feel" it.

## Cluster D — Nutrition (LD03/04) — *The 4-Hour Body* (Ferriss)
**BIO10 — Minimum Effective Dose.** The smallest input that produces the result; more is waste or harm. Applies to supplements, training, and intervention.
**BIO11 — Measure, don't guess.** Track the few inputs that move the needle (protein, fasting window, key biomarkers) — vanity tracking is noise (mirror of Finance F7 transparency).
**BIO12 — Protein floor + glucose discipline.** Adequate protein for the strength substrate; avoid the glucose spikes that wreck the afternoon cognitive block.

## Cluster E — Cold & Hormesis (LD03/05) — *Cold Exposure / BLS*
**BIO13 — Voluntary discomfort builds the tolerance involuntary stress will demand.** Hormetic stress (cold) trains the resilience the business will later require — controlled dose, then recover.
**BIO14 — Discomfort is a trainable skill, not a mood.** Abou's lesson: the discipline of showing up to the hard thing IS the transformation; you don't feel ready, you become ready by repetition.

## Cluster F — Strength & Mobility (LD03/06) — *Bigger Leaner Stronger* + *Supple Leopard*
**BIO15 — Progressive overload or atrophy.** ≥2 resistance sessions/week, one variable progressed weekly — muscle is the organ of longevity and the metabolic sink.
**BIO16 — Position before load (Starrett).** Move well first, load second; any joint <90% ROM gets pre-rehab before it becomes injury. Mobility is daily (10-15min), not occasional.
**BIO17 — Movement is the anti-sedentary floor.** 7,000 steps/day floor + posture resets every 30min — the body is not built for the chair the business creates.

## Cluster G — Learning Acquisition (LD04/07) — *Learning How to Learn* + *Ultralearning*
**BIO18 — Focused + diffuse, alternated.** Deep 45-90min focus blocks, then deliberate diffuse rest — both modes are required for schema formation (Oakley). Beyond 90min = diminishing returns.
**BIO19 — Active recall > re-reading; spaced > massed.** ≥60% of study is retrieval (not passive review); retrieve at 24h/3d/7d/21d. Re-reading feels productive and isn't.
**BIO20 — Interleave, never grind one skill to mastery in isolation.** Mixed practice builds transferable, durable skill.
**BIO21 — Just-In-Time, not Just-In-Case.** Learn to solve a real current problem (mirror of Dan Martell PE/Finance + the Search-First skill) — learning without an en.jeu is cognitive noise.
**BIO22 — A skill must produce a measurable output within 20h (First 20 Hours).** Acquisition without application is forgotten; ship something.

## Cluster H — Cognition Mastery (LD04/08) — *The Art of Learning* (Waitzkin)
**BIO23 — Make smaller circles.** Master the fundamental deeply, then compress — depth of the basic beats breadth of the flashy (the freestyle drilled to reflex = Abou's "performance").
**BIO24 — Investment in loss.** Accept losing while restructuring at a deeper level; the Region-Beta refusal to settle for "good enough" (mirror People PE21 iteration).
**BIO25 — The soft zone / emotional regulation.** Anxiety >7/10 degrades hippocampal encoding — abort the session (AREA_STANDARD §3.2); calm focus, not forced grind.
**BIO26 — Discipline is the bridge across all domains (the Abou principle).** The same repeated discipline that forges the freestyle forges the fight forges the business. Bio is where that discipline is *manufactured*; it then exports to every other Jerry.

## Cluster I — Cross-domain authority (the STOP)
**BIO27 — Bio issues STOP, never GO; LD03→LD04→Beth HALT.** When the substrate degrades, Bio freezes expansion regardless of which Jerry owns the metric (AREA_STANDARD §4 HALT tree). This is the anti-pattern guard: *no Jerry, not even Business, progresses by burning the body.*
**BIO28 — Founder Load Ceiling is a hard gate.** Expansion consumes a finite vitality/cognition pool; >45h/week cognitive load or >3 concurrent execution projects without recovery = automatic ORANGE (AREA_STANDARD §5). Discipline includes the discipline to stop.

- `GO`
- `CONDITIONAL`
- `NO-GO`

| --- | --- |
| People | Host, moderator, follow-up owner |
| IT | Member tooling and access |

| Product | Member value and quality bar |
| Growth | Activation and retention loop |
| Finance | Membership economics |
| Legal | Terms, privacy, community rules |
| Integration | Blockers and unresolved decisions |

## Proof

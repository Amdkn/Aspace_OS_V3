# JTBD-001 - ALIKALY MVP Demo Boundary

## Job

When 04 Alikaly Bana Holding to LLC needs an offer, service package, product module, MVP, demo, or deliverable boundary, produce the Product packet that defines scope, exclusions, acceptance, proof, and cross-domain readiness.

## Output

1. Product/service scope.
2. Exclusions and non-goals.
3. MVP/demo/proof boundary.
4. Acceptance criteria and QA.
5. Ops/IT/Finance/People/Legal handoffs.
6. Sales/Growth promise alignment.
7. Proof path and readiness decision.

## Eight-Domain Product Map

| Domain | Product Surface | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | Market signal and value promise | VOC/ICP and promise evidence |
| Sales | Offer packaging and proposal fit | Scope/exclusions and closeable package |
| Product | Deliverable, MVP/demo, QA | Acceptance criteria and proof boundary |
| Ops | Delivery workflow and support | SOP, handoff, incident path |
| IT | Runtime, tooling, data | System/access/source-of-truth boundary |
| Finance | Unit economics and pricing | Cost, margin, price link |
| People | Builder/reviewer/operator roles | Owner, reviewer, capacity proof |
| Legal | IP, claims, terms, compliance | Ownership and legal boundary |

## Guardrails

- Do not build or sell vague scope.
- Do not let demo imply production readiness.
- Do not skip acceptance criteria.
- Do not detach price from scope.
- Do not bypass Legal/IP or Ops delivery boundaries.
- Project constraint: 
No entity/governance deliverable moves without scope, authority, Legal/Finance/J03 boundary, and record proof.

## Proof

Attach the Product packet, acceptance criteria, demo/proof boundary, cross-domain approvals, and decision log. Until then, Product remains CONDITIONAL.

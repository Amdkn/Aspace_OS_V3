# JTBD-002 ABC Transverse Ops Gate

## Job
When ABC prepares a childcare or OS delivery action, Ops must verify all eight B2 domains have given enough proof for B3 to execute safely.

## Output
A dated Ops readiness decision:
- `GO`
- `CONDITIONAL`
- `NO-GO`

## Readiness Checks
| Domain | Check |
| --- | --- |
| People | Owner, staff capacity, backup |
| IT | Tools, access, records |
| Ops | SOP, schedule, checklist |
| Product | Scope and quality definition |
| Growth | Promise and intake alignment |
| Finance | Cost, price, payment timing |
| Legal | Childcare compliance and risk boundary |
| Integration | Blockers and unresolved decisions |

## Guardrail
No childcare-sensitive action launches without explicit compliance, staffing, and SOP proof.

## Proof
Attach the B2 handoff packet, blocker list, and final Ops decision.

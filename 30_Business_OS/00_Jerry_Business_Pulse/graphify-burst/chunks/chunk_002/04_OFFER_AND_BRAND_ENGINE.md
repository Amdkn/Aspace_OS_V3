# Offer And Brand Engine

## Purpose

Million Dollar Weekend, 100M Offers, and Billion Dollar Brand Club principles are used here as practical pressure tests: find a painful market, make a clear promise, test fast, then build a recognizable category.

## Offer Packet

Every project must define:

| Field | Required Answer |
|---|---|
| ICP | Who has the urgent painful problem? |
| Pain | What costly or emotional problem is being removed? |
| Dream outcome | What result is promised? |
| Time delay | How fast does value appear? |
| Effort/sacrifice | What work is removed from the customer? |
| Risk reversal | What makes saying yes safer? |
| Proof | What evidence supports the claim? |
| Price logic | How does price connect to value and margin? |

## Brand Packet

Every project must also define:

| Field | Required Answer |
|---|---|
| Category | What shelf does this belong on? |
| Enemy | What old way is being replaced? |
| Signature mechanism | What makes the method recognizable? |
| First channel | Where can attention be earned repeatedly? |
| Story asset | What narrative makes the offer memorable? |

## Gate

No project graduates to Business Done without an offer packet and brand packet that Sales, Growth, Product, Finance, and Legal can all inspect.

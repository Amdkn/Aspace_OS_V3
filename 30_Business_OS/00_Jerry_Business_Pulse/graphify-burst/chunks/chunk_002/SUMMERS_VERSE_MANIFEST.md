## ICP Variants — Orbiter Primary, Nexus Secondary

### Orbiter (Field-First / Logistics) — PRIMARY
**Trigger**: Childcare operations = execution/logistics heavy, liability-focused
**Profile**: Clients who buy on capacity, reliability, delivery guarantees, regulatory compliance. Decision driven by track record and compliance assurance.
**Mode markers**: Highest ASP, relationship-driven, contract-heavy, regulatory-bound.
**Primary books**: `Built to Sell` (Warrilow), `The E-Myth Revisited` (Gerber), `Who Not How` (Sullivan)
**Constraint**: Childcare = high liability, strong regulations. Every offer must pass compliance review before launch.

### Nexus (Data-First / Expert Knowledge) — SECONDARY
**Trigger**: Regulatory compliance, credentialing, knowledge systems
**Profile**: Clients who buy on credentials, case studies, compliance certifications. Decision driven by evidence and regulatory standing.
**Mode markers**: Longer sales cycle, compliance documentation, eval-intensive buyers.
**Primary books**: `Expert Secrets` (Canter), `The Consulting Bible` (Maister)
**Constraint**: Childcare operators must meet state/federal licensing. Nexus mode supports credential-first positioning.

|---|---|---|---|

**DRY contract (the heart of the alignment):** a Picard project **points to** the macro doctrine,
never copies it. A project's `03_<DOMAIN>_PRINCIPLES_REF.md` is a one-line pointer to
`…/J01…/B2_Area_Domains/<NN>/03_<HERO>_<DOMAIN>_PRINCIPLES.md` — not a fork. One datum, one owner
(ADR-MESH-L2-001). Project learning hardens Jerry doctrine **only after field proof**, never from assumption.

## 5. Graduation & Product-Isolation rules (the gates Picard enforces)

A Summer's Verse **graduates** only when (register): 1. B1 has 1Y/3Y/10Y vision. 2. Four 12WY cycles
have named Summer responsibility. 3. B2 domains own Rocks + tactic specs. 4. B3 produces Lead/Lag logs.
5. Data receives archive-ready evidence. 6. The **B2 gate matrix** reads PASS/CONDITIONAL/BLOCKED for
all 8 domains.

**Product-Isolation Rule:** a project is **not** operational just because Product has a working
prototype. Until Growth, Sales, Product, Ops, IT, Finance, People, Legal all have explicit gate status,
the project is `PRODUCT_ONLY_PROTOTYPE`. (Micro mirror of the macro "Product Done ≠ Business Done".)
